OUTFIT: a slavic-folk tunic blouse, a traditional pinafore overdress, and a woven belt. The undergarment is a muted red tunic blouse with a loose, relaxed fit. It features long, voluminous bishop sleeves gathered tightly at the wrists into narrow cuffs. The shoulders and upper sleeves display horizontal band of dark red and black geometric patterns that encircle the entire fabric. The overgarment is a muted dark forest green sarafan pinafore dress. It features a high, gathered empire waist bodice supported by wide, square-set shoulder straps trimmed with a black and red patterned border. The skirt is floor-length, A-line, and heavily gathered at the high waist, flaring into wide folds. A thick horizontal border of dark red and black striped folk patterns trims the bottom hem. Secured tightly around the high waist is a wide, textured woven belt in dark red. It has two long, dangling tassels with frayed ends that hang down the front of the skirt.
