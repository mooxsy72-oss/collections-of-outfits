OUTFIT: ensemble belongs to a fantasy clothing tradition, drawing inspiration from high-fantasy warrior-goddess motifs. The dominant color is metallic gold, paired with a secondary sheer cream or off-white hue.The primary structural silhouette is defined by an ultra-fitted overbust corset bodysuit constructed from rigid, reflective metallic gold lamé with vertical boning channels. It features molded underwire cups, a front lace-up closure with gold cording, and an integrated high-cut brief section that exposes the thighs and hips.Layered over the bodysuit is an split-front overskirt train made of lightweight, sheer, and volumetrically gathered cream tulle or organza. The fabric has high shape retention, creating extreme volume at the hips before draping into a floor-length train. The tulle is heavily scattered with metallic gold leaf flecks and dense speckles.The sleeves are detached, statement puff sleeves made of the same gold-flecked sheer tulle. They feature exaggerated volume at the upper arm via tight gathers at the shoulder, cinched tightly with a gold band before cascading down to a fitted forearm cuff.Footwear consists of structured, metallic gold over-the-knee boots with a tight lace-up front closure.Accessories include a chunky, rigid gold metallic choker necklace decorated with intricate filigree details and dangling chain fringe.
