OUTFIT: technical streetwear outfit relies on a monochromatic black palette, layered construction, and full body coverage. The look balances a structured, high-volume upper torso with streamlined lower layers, completely free of surface patterns.The outermost layer is a matte black technical shell jacket. It features a loose, boxy silhouette with an attached, rigid hood and a high-fastening collar. The fabric is a crisp, medium-weight waterproof synthetic material that retains its angular shape. Distinctive details include two asymmetrical, oversized flapped chest utility pockets, wide sleeves with adjustable cuffs, and long, hanging zipper pulls and straps extending from the lower hem.Beneath the shell is a lightweight black insulated mid-layer jacket. It has a slimmer fit, a high crewneck, a snap-button front closure, and a straight hem that aligns with the outer shell jacket.The trousers are relaxed-fit black technical cargo pants crafted from structured, mid-weight nylon fabric. They feature an elastic waistband with a contrasting white drawstring, deep inset pockets at the hips, and wide legs that taper sharply into elasticated, gathered cuffs at the ankles.The footwear consists of low-top sneakers in black suede and canvas. They feature a prominent white leather swoosh logo on the side panels, a white heel tab, and flat white laces over a black tongue. The shoes are grounded by a distressed, vintage-finish off-white rubber midsole and outsole.
