OUTFIT: monochrome, solid black ensemble leans into a futuristic, tactical cyberpunk stage-performance aesthetic, contrasting an extremely cropped, minimal upper layer with voluminous, structured lower garments. The outfit reveals significant areas of the body, exposing the entire midriff, arms, and a large vertical section of the right leg.The upper piece is an ultra-cropped, sleeveless tactical vest with an integrated high mock neckline. It features rigid, heavy-weight canvas construction with functional front webbing, plastic clips, and a side-buckle adjustment that keeps it firmly anchored above the ribs. Twin black elastic straps wrap around the bicep as independent accessories, paired with a studded leather cuff on the opposite wrist.The lower silhouette is dominated by high-waisted, oversized cargo trousers made from stiff, durable cotton twill. They feature a wide-leg cut, large side pockets, and are cinched at the waist with a thick black leather belt. The right leg features a dramatic, intentional high-thigh side slit running from the hip to the ankle, revealing a built-in black elastic thigh garter with a silver slide buckle. Long tactical suspender straps hang loosely from the vest to the waistband. The look finishes with heavy-duty black combat boots featuring thick rubber platform lug soles.
