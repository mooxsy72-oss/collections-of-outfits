OUTFIT: outfit presents a highly tailored, monochromatic tailoring composition heavily inspired by late 1980s power dressing. The dominant color palette is a neutral mix of tan, beige, and dark brown, arranged in a micro-scale houndstooth or grid pattern that covers the entire main suit. The main visual focus is drawn to the exaggerated, structural shoulders contrasted against a dramatically cinched-in waistline.The primary outerwear layer is a single-breasted blazer made from a heavyweight, structured wool-blend suiting fabric with high shape retention. It features a sharp, elongated silhouette with highly padded, rigid, and wide shoulders that create a boxy upper frame. The blazer cinches drastically at the natural waist before flaring slightly over the hips, ending in a straight, mid-thigh length hemline. It is detailed with deep notched lapels forming a plunging V-neckline, long fitted sleeves, two horizontal flap pockets at the hips, and three dark brown central buttons. Underneath, a matching miniskirt in the identical tan patterned suiting fabric provides the lower-body layer, featuring a straight, form-fitting silhouette that ends a few inches below the blazer's hemline. Transparent, ultra-sheer dark brown hosiery covers the legs.Footwear consists of dark brown leather slingback pumps. They feature a sharp, pointed toe box, a deep V-shaped vamp cut, and a thin, towering stiletto heel with a delicate strap wrapping around the heel.Accessories include a structured, trapezoidal dark brown leather handbag carried by a short top handle, and chunky, dome-shaped metallic gold button earrings worn at the ears.
