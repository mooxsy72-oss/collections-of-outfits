OUTFIT: layered, textured ensemble revolves around a rich burgundy color palette contrasted against stark white and patterned red elementmonochromatic ensemble centers on a rich, deep burgundy color palette, varying only in texture, opacity, and material weight. The look is anchored by a form-fitting sheer dress, layered with a structured leather jacket and matching knee-high boots, creating a balanced interplay between soft, draped textiles and rigid, protective outerwear.The primary full-body garment is a fitted mini dress crafted from an ultra-lightweight, sheer burgundy mesh or fine-knit fabric. The dress features a high, draped turtleneck collar and long, slim sleeves that bunch slightly at the wrists, extending past the jacket cuffs. It hugs the torso and waist closely, ending in a straight, sheer hemline at the mid-thigh to offer a high degree of leg exposure.Layered over the dress is a dark burgundy or deep brown leather jacket. This outerwear piece has a relaxed, slightly boxy silhouette with soft, unpadded shoulders and long, straight sleeves. It features a simple folded shirt-style collar, a straight zip-front closure left open, and a straight hem that aligns near the hemline of the sheer inner dress. The leather has a matte, supple finish with soft folding.The footwear consists of knee-high boots in a matching dark burgundy hue. They feature a structured, smooth leather or suede shaft that fits cleanly over the calves, a sharply pointed toe, and a high stiletto heel.
