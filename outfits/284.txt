OUTFIT: color pallete is deep burgundy and crisp white with gold accents. The silhouette centers on a highly structured, corseted bodice contrasting with a voluminous, short bell-shaped skirt.The fitted bodice is crafted from heavy, stiff burgundy velvet that retains a sharp shape. It features a low, wide square neckline trimmed with metallic gold lace and intricate gold baroque embroidery. A large fabric rose sits at the center bust. The three-quarter sleeves are moderately puffy at the shoulders before tapering, finished with gold-embroidered cuffs holding large faux gemstones.The ultra-short, multi-layered skirt creates massive volume through stiff petticoats or structural crinoline. The top layer is a white, heavy-weight satin or faille fabric featuring trailing gold vine embroidery and dimensional, scattered red fabric roses. The hemline is finished with dense, tiered white floral lace ruffles.Accessories include matching pearl-and-gold beaded drop earrings, a multi-strand pearl choker necklace with a central ruby pendant, stacked pearl bracelets, and a coordinating gold rose ring. Over-the-knee structured white leather boots cover the lower legs, leaving the mid-thighs exposed.
