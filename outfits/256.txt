OUTFIT: high-fantasy gown evokes an ethereal, elven aesthetic. It features an extremely deconstructed, floor-length silhouette that balances a heavily exposed, cage-like midriff with voluminous draping below. The palette is strictly neutral, mixing cream, beige, and stark white fabrics.The top is a gathered cream halter bodice with a deep plunge neckline, supported by ultra-thin cord straps that connect to an intricate corded harness across the torso. This harness forms a geometric diamond pattern over the bare midriff. The sides of the hips are entirely exposed, framed only by a series of parallel, curved horizontal cording loops that connect the front and back panels. Small silver metallic beads adorn the central harness cords.The lower half consists of layered, asymmetrical skirts. The front layer features sheer, lightweight beige chiffon that drapes softly in fluid, cascading folds from a low-slung, central V-point. Underneath and extending to the sides is a thick, opaque white fabric with heavy structure, pooling into a dramatic, wide train on the floor. At the neck, a wide cream fabric choker sits tight against the throat, layered with a multi-strand silver ball-chain necklace.
