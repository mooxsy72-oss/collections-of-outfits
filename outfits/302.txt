OUTFIT: outfit presents a monochromatic, hyper-feminine ensemble in a solid cream-white palette, heavily blending structured tailoring with soft, playful shapes reminiscent of a modern co-ord set. The color is distributed uniformly across all pieces, creating a cohesive, elongated look. Visual focus is drawn to the open midriff and the structural contrast between the sharp, geometric lines of the upper torso and the soft flounces of the skirt.The upper-body garment is a structured, cropped jacket-style top made from a heavyweight, smooth woven fabric with excellent shape retention. It features a fitted bodice that terminates high above the waist in a straight, horizontal cropped hem. The neckline is round and collarless, secured at the throat by a single large, self-covered cream button. The shoulders are highly structured and padded, flowing into voluminous, elbow-length puff sleeves that taper slightly at the cuffs.The lower-body garment is a high-waisted tiered miniskirt. It begins with a wide, smooth, and heavily structured waistband that dips into a sharp, elongated V-shape at the center front, mirroring a corset-like construction. Below this yoke, the skirt transitions into three layers of lightweight, fluidly draped ruffles that cascade down to mid-thigh, creating a flounced, airy silhouette.Footwear consists of towering, knee-high cream leather platform boots. They feature a chunky, block high heel, a thick platform sole, a rounded toe box, and a front lace-up design running the full length of the stiff, form-fitting shaft.
