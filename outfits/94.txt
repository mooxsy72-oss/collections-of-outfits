OUTFIT: modern fantasy look integrates traditional East Asian construction, blending a classic mandarin-collared shirt silhouette with dramatic, romantic poetic sleeves. The color palette is dominated by a vibrant scarlet-red top, grounded by secondary matte black trousers and metallic gold detailing.The focal garment is a lightweight, fluidly draping silk or satin shirt in vivid scarlet-red, providing complete upper body coverage. It features a high, stiffened mandarin collar and a central frog-closure plaquet running down the chest. The sleeves are intentionally exaggerated, oversized, because of the sleeves the shoulders look very wide visually, and the waist is narrow. Sleeves are voluminous, gathering heavily at the shoulders and ballooning outward before tapering into slim cuffs tied with delicate red ribbons at the wrists. Intricate, large-scale metallic gold embroidery featuring traditional floral and organic motifs is placed symmetrically along the center chest placket, shoulders, and outer forearms. A thick, matching gold embroidered band defines the shirt's lower hem, which is tucked neatly into the waistband.The lower half consists of slim-fit, tailored black trousers made from a mid-weight, structured twill or leather-look fabric that retains a sharp line down the legs. They are paired with a thick black leather belt featuring an ornate, rounded gold buckle. Suspended from the right belt loop is a fine gold wallet chain ending in a long metallic tassel. The outfit concludes with polished black leather ankle boots featuring an elongated, pointed toe and a slight western-style cuban heel.
