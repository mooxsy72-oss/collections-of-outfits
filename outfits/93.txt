OUTFIT: fantasy regalia draws inspiration from European monarchical court dress and military uniforms, indicated by a structured tunic, ornate collar insignia, orders, and a dramatic floor-sweeping coronation mantle. The dominant color is solid black, contrasted by an off-white inner lining and a vibrant crimson-red sash, all heavily accented with metallic gold.The primary garment is a tailored, waist-length black military tunic made from a structured, heavy wool fabric that retains rigid shape. It features a high, stiff mandarin collar adorned with gold filigree and a voluminous white fabric cravat at the throat. The straight-cut long sleeves are detailed with tiered gold braided bands at the cuffs and white fabric swags gathered at the shoulders. Matching black, slim-cut dress trousers offer full body coverage, tapering into polished black leather dress shoes.A massive, heavy black velvet mantle loops over the left shoulder, pooling on the floor behind. It is lined with a smooth, fluidly draping off-white satin fabric that contrasts sharply with the dark exterior. A wide crimson-red fabric sash drapes from the right shoulder across the torso to the left hip, ending in two large, dense metallic gold tassels. Additional accessories include a heavy gold livery chain with a central sunburst medallion across the chest, two tiered gold order stars pinned below the left breast, and a gold-hilted ceremonial sword suspended at the left hip.
