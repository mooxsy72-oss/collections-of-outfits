OUTFIT: a futuristic sci-fi fantasy ensemble combines liquid-metal body armor with deconstructed drapery. The monochrome palette consists of metallic chrome silver and matte concrete gray, featuring organic, fluid textures.The top is an ultra-minimalist, undersized metallic bra piece resembling melted chrome bands wrapping under the bust, completely revealing the skin of the shoulders, midriff, upper chest, and back. The lower body is covered by a high-waisted, floor-length column skirt. This garment features a dual-layer waist construction: a tight, light silver structural inner layer exposed at the left hip, over which a heavy, dark gray fabric is bundled and rolled into a thick, asymmetric waistband. The skirt has a stiff, crumpled drape that pools around the feet, incorporating shredded elements and silver liquid-metal trim that drips down the front and hemline. Extending from the upper body are full-length gauntlets or body paint replicating molten metal, completely covering the arms up to the shoulders with sharp, spiked protrusions rising past the collarbones. The rigid, reflective armor components dominate the upper silhouette, while the heavy, volumetric fabric of the skirt defines the lower body, creating a striking contrast between liquid structure and raw textile.
