OUTFIT: a modern streetwear outfit is heavily influenced by the contemporary Y2K and Harajuku subculture aesthetics, prioritizing exaggerated proportions and whimsical cartoon themes. The color distribution balances light bubblegum pink and a medium-wash light blue denim, anchored by accents of white and black.
The upper garment is an intentionally oversized, boxy t-shirt with a loose, draping fit that falls past the hips. It features a simple crew neckline trimmed with a contrasting black band. The drop-shoulder short sleeves are unusually wide and elongated, extending down to the elbows. Two parallel black athletic stripes run from the neck down the shoulders and sleeves. Centered on the chest is a large screen-printed illustration of a cartoon cat with a red bow, accompanied by text. The lower garment consists of highly voluminous, baggy cargo-style jeans crafted from rigid, structured denim. The pants feature an elasticized high-rise waistband, giving way to an extremely wide-leg silhouette that bunches drastically at the ankles. Utility cargo pockets with buttoned flaps are stitched onto the sides of both thighs. The denim is covered in medium-to-large scale pink, black, and white graffiti-style graphics, including cartoon bear heads, hearts, and words like "STAY HAPPY" and "SMILE" scattered unevenly down both pant legs.  The footwear consists of chunky, low-top white leather sneakers with thick platform rubber soles. A hand-painted cartoon cat face with a prominent red bow accents the outer side of each shoe.
