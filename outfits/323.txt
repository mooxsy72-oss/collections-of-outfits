OUTFIT: full-body, high-volume gown features a rich, royal color palette dominated by deep magenta-burgundy and dusty rose, accented by cream lace. The visual composition splits attention between the heavily embellished, fitted bodice and an oversized, dramatically structured bell-shaped skirt filled with cascading ruffles, metallic gold embroidery, and dimensional floral clusters.The outfit belongs to a fantasy-historical clothing tradition heavily inspired by 18th-century Rococo court dress (Robe à la Française or Robe à l'Anglaise), supported by its wide, conical pannier-style silhouette, low square neckline, stomacher-style central bodice panel, and pagoda-style tiered lace sleeves.The primary full-body garment consists of a tightly tailored bodice and an expansive, floor-sweeping ballgown skirt. The bodice features a wide, square neckline framed by ruffled cream lace and artificial dusty rose rosettes across the bust. Constructed from structured, reflective magenta-burgundy satin, it tapers to a sharp, elongated V-point at the waist. The three-quarter-length sleeves feature voluminous, tiered pagoda cuffs made of layered dusty rose gauze and scalloped cream lace, decorated with small satin ribbons.The exaggerated dome silhouette of the skirt is achieved through an internal hoop or pannier framework. The overskirt consists of heavy, gathered magenta-burgundy satin that parts in an inverted "V" to reveal a highly decorated petticoat insert. This central panel features multi-layered, diagonally draped tiers of dusty rose tulle and lace ruffles, densely detailed with metallic gold filigree embroidery at the hem. Clusters of dimensional fabric roses, silk ribbons, and gathered rosettes line the split edges and populate the lower center skirt.The accessory is a multi-strand pearl choker necklace, featuring three close-fitting rows of uniform white pearls with small teardrop pearl pendants suspended along the bottom strand.
