OUTFIT: avant-garde ensemble features a highly structured, layered denim bodysuit and cropped jacket composition, dominated by deep indigo blue, and paired with thigh-high metallic silver boots that command equal visual attention.The base is a form-fitting denim bodysuit with a sweetheart neckline and pinstripe stitching, heavily cinched by two thick, western-buckled denim waist belts. Cascading from the high-cut leg openings are multiple asymmetric, decorative denim belt straps with ornate silver western hardware. Over this, a structured, ultra-cropped denim jacket with a raw, curved hem features oversized drop-shoulder sleeves lined with long, dense metallic silver fringe hanging from the underarms. A matching denim choker collar with double buckles secures around the neck, leaving the upper chest largely exposed. The denim throughout is heavy, rigid, and holds its dramatic architectural shape.Below, towering thigh-high boots in a sparkling, textured metallic silver fabric fit closely to the legs, ending in pointed toes. The visual volume is heavily concentrated in the upper torso and shoulders through the wide jacket silhouette and layered belts, contrasting with the sleek, shimmering vertical lines of the boots. All metallic elements, from the belt buckles to the fringe and boot fabric, create a cohesive, glitzy western-futurism theme. No hats.
