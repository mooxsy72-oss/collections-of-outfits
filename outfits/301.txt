OUTFIT: outfit presents an edgy, contemporary ensemble that blends structured academic elements with a bold rocker aesthetic. The dominant color is solid black, contrasted by a crisp secondary pop of bright white at the center of the torso. Visual focus is drawn to the exaggerated, oversized volume of the outerwear contrasted against the ultra-short, form-fitting silhouette of the lower half.The base upper layer is a classic white dress shirt made from a lightweight, smooth woven cotton fabric. It features a structured, sharp pointed collar buttoned completely closed to the throat. Tucked into the waistband, it provides an anchor for the outerwear layer, which is an oversized, heavyweight black leather motorcycle jacket. This jacket features dropped shoulders, loose-fitting wide sleeves with silver zipper details at the cuffs, large notched lapels with silver snap hardware, and asymmetrical front zipper tracks. The jacket hangs loosely to mid-thigh, creating a boxy and dominant visual volume.The lower-body garment consists of high-waisted black leather micro-shorts. Crafted from smooth, mid-weight leather with high shape retention, they feature a tight, form-fitting silhouette that ends high on the thigh with a straight hemline. Ultra-sheer, fine black tights cover the legs.Footwear consists of structured, knee-high black leather boots. They feature a flat or low-block heel, a rounded toe box, a stiff shaft that retains its shape, and a silver buckle strap accent wrapping around the outer ankle.Accessories include a solid, matte black wide necktie knotted tightly at the white collar and draping straight down the chest. Oval-shaped sunglasses with thick, glossy white frames and dark lenses rest on the face, paired with small, delicate silver drop earrings.
