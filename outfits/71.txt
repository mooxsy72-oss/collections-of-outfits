OUTFIT: bridal-fantasy ensemble features an ultra-embellished, monochromatic white-and-silver palette characterized by a rigid, heavily textured bodice and a asymmetrical, trailing lace lower half.The primary garment is a tightly fitted, structured corset-style bodice with a sweetheart neckline. It is heavily reinforced, showing high shape retention, and is dense with layered floral lace, silver sequins, dimensional embroidery, and a prominent silver butterfly motif centered on the bust. The midriff and upper torso are fully covered, but the shoulders, neck, and upper chest are exposed.The lower garment consists of a high-low skirt with an integrated lace-accented short base. The front exposes the thighs completely, while the sides and back trail into a long, voluminous, semi-sheer floor-length train. This skirt is constructed from lightweight, fluid white floral lace panels, gathered thickly at the hips to create outward volume. It is further layered with draped swags of fine white pearl strands across the front hip area.An intricate, separate shoulder-and-neck harness accessory connects a high lace collar to the bodice via radial pearl string lines. This harness extends outward to support structured, dimensional lace butterflies resting like epaulets over the otherwise bare upper arms.
