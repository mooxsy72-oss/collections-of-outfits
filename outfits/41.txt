OUTFIT: The casual streetwear outfit follows a dark color scheme dominated by deep navy blue and solid black, punctuated by white accents.The centerpiece is an oversized, heavyweight knit track jacket that synthesizes athletic wear with traditional East Asian aesthetics. Its design directly borrows elements from the traditional Tang suit, featuring five pairs of white frog button toggle closures along the front instead of a standard zipper. The jacket incorporates an elongated stand collar, slouchy drop-shoulder sleeves with a three-stripe white pattern running down the sides, and a gathered elastic hem creating a voluminous, rounded shape.Layered underneath is a slim-fit, crew-neck tank top made of thin, ribbed black cotton jersey that contrasts sharply with the bulk of the outerwear.The lower half is dominated by ultra-baggy balloon-style sweatpants. The black, heavy fleece fabric mimics the extreme, gathered volume of traditional martial arts trousers, using a high drawstring waist and tightly cinched elastic cuffs to structure the extreme width.Classic low-top black canvas sneakers with white laces, white toe caps, and white rubber soles anchor the base.
