OUTFIT: ensemble represents a highly stylized, modern interpretation of a classic fantasy witch or sorceress archetype. The visual composition is completely monochromatic, utilizing variations of deep black and dark charcoal across all garments, with metallic silver and brass elements offering stark accents. Visual attention is strongly drawn to the stark contrast between the heavy, structured leather-like upper garments and the highly exposed thighs, framed by a massive pointed hat and a voluminous, flowing cape.The upper-body consists of a form-fitting, highly structured black corset bodice with a low, supportive sweetheart neckline. The front panel features a tight criss-cross lacing system, while the fabric has a subtle sheen reminiscent of polished leather or heavy vinyl. For the lower-body, the mannequin wears tight-fitting, ultra-short black tailored shorts that hug the hips, featuring a straight-cut hem and a smooth, matte fabric texture. Wrapping the arms are fitted, forearm-length black bracers or fingerless gauntlets made of dark leather, accented with polished silver metal plates resting on the upper forearms.The primary piece of outerwear is a full-length, lightweight black fabric cape that rests broad on the shoulders. It fastens at the throat with a dual-strap leather collar detailed with silver metallic buckles, cascading down past the knees in a fluid drape with a raw, slightly tattered or pointed hemline. On the feet are thigh-high, form-fitting black leather boots. The right boot features a contrasting white or light-grey upper band and is heavily secured around the upper thigh by three thick leather straps with silver buckles, while the lower boot maintains a sleek, stacked appearance down to a sturdy, flat sole. Accessories include a wide-brimmed leather utility belt in a contrasting warm brown tone, complete with a large brass buckle and an attached embossed leather pouch on the right hip. Crowning the ensemble is an oversized, structured black witch’s hat with an exceptionally wide, flat circular brim and a tall, naturally creased, and crumpled crown tapering to a point.
