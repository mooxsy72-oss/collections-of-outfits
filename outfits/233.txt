OUTFIT: historical fantasy costume draws from early medieval and Norse aesthetics, combining a tunica, braccae, and a large animal pelt. The palette relies entirely on earth tones, featuring dominant shades of brown, tan, and cream, with full body coverage.The most visually prominent element is an oversized, bulky fur cloak made from thick, long-pile animal pelt in mottled brown, tan, and cream. It drapes heavily over both shoulders and upper arms, framing a high collar.Beneath sits a tan, loose-fitting cotton or linen tunic. It has a high neckline, a gathered torso, and wide sleeves that bunch heavily down the arms, terminating in a straight hem.The trousers are dark brown, relaxed-fit woolen hosen or braccae. They feature a loose, ballooning silhouette through the thighs that tapers sharply below the knee.The footwear consists of knee-high, rugged brown leather turnshoes with wrap-around leather laces crisscrossing the shins and calves.A wide, thick brown leather kidney belt secures the waist with a simple metallic buckle. This belt supports a long, dark brown leather sword scabbard hanging diagonally across the leg and a horizontally mounted bone-handled seax knife. Rigid, matching brown leather cuffs encase both wrists.
