OUTFIT: outfit draws heavily from the theatrical Harlequin, Pierrot, and classic circus clown traditions, defined by its exaggerated ruffs, voluminous striped sleeves, and structured conical headpiece.The silhouette is dominated by an ultra-voluminous upper body and flared lower half, contrasting with a tightly fitted midsection. A strapless, structured corset with a sweetheart neckline exposes the upper chest and shoulders. It is crafted from heavy, rigid tapestry fabric featuring a multi-colored floral pattern on a dark background. Layered over the shoulders is an oversized, multi-layered white fabric neck ruff that retains a stiff, pleated structure. Separate, exaggeratedly voluminous balloon sleeves in a wide black-and-white vertical stripe pattern cover the arms, gathered tightly at the upper arm and forearm with olive green fabric ties.The lower half consists of a short, high-waisted bubble skirt in a smooth, mid-weight olive green satin with a soft, reflective sheen. The skirt flares out from the waist to mid-thigh, holding a rounded shape via deep gathers. Draped across the front waist is a secondary accent accessory: a double-strand hip chain made of large, metallic gold spherical beads. The legs are partially exposed from mid-thigh to the knee, where they meet slouchy, knee-high white leather boots featuring a wide, structured shaft with visible soft folds. A tall, rigid, fabric-covered conical hat in a pale metallic gold hue sits atop the head, completing the dramatic proportions.
