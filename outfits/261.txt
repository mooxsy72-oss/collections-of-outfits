OUTFIT: fantasy ensemble features an ethereal, fairy-inspired aesthetic dominated entirely by monochromatic bright white. The main garment is a structured, ultra-mini corset dress with a fitted, panelled bodice, thin spaghetti straps, and a straight, low-cut neckline. It features a strategic midriff cutout directly below the bust line. The waist drops sharply into a voluminous, tiered skirt with heavy gathering and asymmetrical, handkerchief-style ruffles that flare outward. The rigid fabric is a lightweight eyelet cotton blend that holds structural shape while maintaining a delicate, micro-patterned texture. This short-length piece leaves the shoulders, collarbones, chest, arms, lower midriff, and thighs completely exposed. Serving as the primary visual focal point are massive, oversized fairy wings attached at the back. They feature a rigid, metallic silver filigree framework inlaid with sheer, iridescent fabric that mimics real insect wings. The wings extend far beyond the body width, dramatically altering the natural silhouette. The look is accessorized with a small, structured white leather top-handle handbag with a trapezoidal shape, sharp geometric lines, and a front flap closure. Single bracelets accent the wrist.
