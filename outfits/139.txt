OUTFIT: outfit blends elements of East Asian traditional dress (specifically Hanfu and traditional northern nomadic attire) with mystical creature motifs, characterized by heavy layering, voluminous silhouettes, and ornate, symmetrical detailing. The dominant color is a warm cream-ivory, paired with secondary stark white fur and dark red accents.The silhouette is dominated by an oversized, ankle-length outer robe-coat crafted from thick, structured cream-colored wool or faux suede that retains an A-line shape. It features extreme visual volume generated by a massive, plush white faux fur trim lining the oversized collar, lapels, cuffs, and hem. The sleeves are exceptionally wide and elongated, ending in dense fur cuffs. Underneath sits a multi-layered, floor-length inner robe/skirt ensemble with a cross-collar neckline and a wide, dark decorative waistband compressing the core. The entire lower skirt and outer sleeve cuffs are heavily embellished with intricate, medium-density traditional scrollwork and cloud-like embroidery in dark red.Accessories include matching wrapped, calf-high cream boots with cross-straps, hair pendants with metallic beads and tassels, and a prominent, floor-sweeping plush white faux fur tail attached at the back waist. The outfit provides full body coverage from neck to feet.
