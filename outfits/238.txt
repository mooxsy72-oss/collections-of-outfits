OUTFIT: ensemble merges Japanese Sweet Lolita and Coquette styles into a whimsical, animal-themed fantasy bunny outfit. The color distribution is dominated by off-white, with dusty pink as a prominent secondary color, featuring contrasting textures of matte leather, dense feathers, and delicate ruffled lace.The core piece is a tightly fitted, bone-structured corset romper with vertical dusty pink ribbon strips along the torso. It features a square neckline, ruffled lace cap sleeves, and a multi-layered, voluminous lace tutu skirt that flares dramatically from the cinched waist, leaving the lower thighs completely exposed.Accessories include a pink-and-white plush headband with tall, upright feathered bunny ears. A thick, structured off-white ruffled lace collar chokes the neck. The arms feature elbow-length dusty pink fingerless gloves topped with voluminous, oversized cuffs of pink marabou feathers.On the legs, thigh-high white matte leather boots feature front corset-style lacing, stiletto heels, and pink pointed toes. The boots are heavily decorated with three separate tiers of oversized, fluffy pink feather trims wrapping around the upper thighs, calves, and lower ankles, heavily dominating the lower silhouette.
