OUTFIT: outfit features a coordinated, cozy-academic aesthetic anchored by an earthy color palette of dark brown and warm beige. The visual composition blends structured tailoring with delicate textures, drawing primary visual focus to the contrasting plaid pattern on the upper body and the bright white accents framing the waist, cuffs, and boots.The base upper-body layer is a simple white tank top or camisole, over which is layered an oversized, relaxed-fit plaid button-down blouse. Made from a lightweight, fluid woven fabric, the blouse features a muted dark brown, beige, and white plaid pattern. It is styled with an open collar forming a relaxed V-neckline and voluminous, draped sleeves that gather into wide cuffs finished with a thick trim of white floral lace. The lower-body layering consists of a short white under-skirt or lining that peeks out uniformly beneath a high-waisted, dark brown mini skirt. Crafted from a structured, mid-weight suiting fabric, this outer skirt features thick box pleats that flare slightly into an A-line shape. The lower legs are covered in sheer, dark brown or smoky grey hosiery.Footwear consists of structured, knee-high riding boots in a smooth, matte dark brown leather, featuring a flat sole and a rounded toe. Peeking out over the top of the boots are thick, textured white leg warmers or boot socks with a prominent ribbed and ruffled upper edge. The look is accessorized with a slim, smooth white leather belt with a small silver buckle cinching the waist, and a structured white leather shoulder bag with a wide strap draped over the left shoulder.
