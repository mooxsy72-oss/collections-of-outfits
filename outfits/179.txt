OUTFIT: post-apocalyptic, utilitarian survival outfit relies on heavy layering, distressed textures, and muted tones. The dominant colors are earthy grays, desaturated olive, and dark charcoal, presenting a worn, monochromatic palette with no decorative patterns.An oversized, short-sleeved denim button-up shirt dominates the upper silhouette. It features a traditional pointed collar, dropped shoulders, raw frayed sleeve hems, and dual chest patch pockets. The mid-weight, stiff fabric drapes rigidly, showing realistic distressing like bloodstains on the right breast and micro-tears. Layered underneath is a lightweight, form-fitting cotton t-shirt with a stretched crew neckline and a faded olive-gray color. The shirt hems extend past the hips, completely covering the torso and upper arms while leaving the forearms exposed.The lower body is defined by slim-fit, mid-rise jeans in a faded charcoal-brown hue. They conform tightly to the legs down to the ankles, featuring a small distressed rip on the left thigh. On the feet are classic canvas high-top sneakers with contrasting white rubber toe caps, white midsoles, and dark, heavily soiled uppers.A utilitarian accessory completes the look: a structured, dark gray tactical backpack with thick, padded shoulder straps that compress the shirt fabric at the shoulders, anchoring the layered silhouette.
