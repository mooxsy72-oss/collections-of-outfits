OUTFIT: multi-layered ensemble draws from traditional Chinese Hanfu silhouettes blended with a fantasy herbalist or scholar aesthetic. The composition relies on deep black and forest green base layers accented by a flowing, light-cream outer robe.The primary base garment is a structured, long-sleeved black shirt featuring an ornate, pointed down-turned collar detailed with gold leaf embroidery. Wrapped over the torso is a forest green fabric wrap with deep, structured pleats that drape diagonally past the knees, printed with a dense, fine-scale gold bamboo leaf pattern. Layered beneath these panels is a fluid, floor-length matte black pleated skirt providing volume and complete lower-body coverage.The defining silhouette is created by an oversized, lightweight cream outer robe with dramatically wide, floor-grazing bell sleeves. The robe features an open front, drops straight to a pooling hemline, and is adorned along the edges and sleeves with a delicate, sparse bamboo embroidery.The waist is tightly bound by a thick, dark brown leather belt system supporting a complex array of thematic accessories: a dark brown leather satchel with scroll loops, dangling skeleton keys, and a prominent crimson silk tassel. Across the upper chest hangs a decorative antiqued silver metal chain necklace strung with small, clear glass apothecary potion bottles. The lower legs are anchored by structured, knee-high black leather boots adorned with intricate gold brocade embroidery across the shaft and counter.
