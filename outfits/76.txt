OUTFIT: The gown is dominated by an integrated, heavily structured corset-style bodice that transitions into a voluminous, floor-length A-line skirt. The bodice features a square neckline, structured vertical boning channels, and a horizontally ruched center chest panel. The shoulder construction exhibits intentionally exaggerated, oversized puff sleeves with dense, gathered volume at the cap, which narrows below the bicep into tight-fitting, full-length undersleeves.The upper chest, collarbones, and neck are exposed, while the rest of the body is fully covered. The dominant material is a thick, mid-weight metallic silver lamé fabric with a crushed texture that holds crisp structural shapes in the puffed sleeves and creates heavy, structured folds in the draped skirt. The tight undersleeves are constructed from a contrasting, translucent silver floral lace.Accessories include matching metallic silver fingerless gloves that mimic armored gauntlets with segmented panels. A wide, matching silver lace choker covers the neck, complemented by a delicate metallic chain necklace featuring a single drop pendant.
