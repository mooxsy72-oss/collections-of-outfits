OUTFIT: fantasy cyborg outfit blends elements of futuristic mecha design with a Gothic Maid aesthetic.The silhouette is dominated by an exaggerated, form-fitting black mechanical bodysuit that mimics a corseted leotard with a short, stiffly pleated peplum skirt. The bodysuit features high-gloss, rigid molded panels covering the torso, hips, and mechanical high-heeled boots. It exposes the upper thighs and shoulders, creating a stark contrast against white, segmented upper-arm armor.Flowing behind the figure is an oversized, voluminous white lace capelet that drapes from the elbows to the floor. This sheer, lightweight fabric adds dramatic movement, featuring dense floral embroidery along its scalloped borders.The dominant color is glossy black, contrasted by stark white armor plating and the flowing white lace cape. Ornate gold filigree trims and mechanical dials accent the hip joints, collar, and footwear. Visually dominant accessories include thigh-high, segmented cybernetic greaves with integrated stiletto heels, held up by thin structural garter straps. A complex, multi-segmented lower-face respirator mask features intricate paneling, exposed wiring, circular nodes, and metallic venting. This mask physically integrates with a cybernetic headset featuring large, stylized robotic cat ears. These angular, geometric ear structures are crafted from rigid, polished dark metallic plating, accented by glowing red circular rings near the temple joints.
