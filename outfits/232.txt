OUTFIT: barbarian-style ensemble utilizes elements from fictional prehistoric and Germanic warrior tropes. The outfit prioritizes minimal body coverage, leaving the entire torso, shoulders, and arms completely exposed. The color palette relies on natural, earth-toned colors including muted charcoal gray, deep brown, and tan.An asymmetrical chest harness serves as the main upper element, featuring a thick, rugged brown leather strap worn diagonally across the torso. This strap anchors a large, voluminous mantle of long-pile, multi-toned tan and gray faux fur sitting over the right shoulder.The waist area features a complex, heavily layered kilt assembly. A jagged, tattered charcoal-gray linen underskirt acts as the base. Over it sits a stiff, vertically draped center loincloth paneled in smooth, heavy olive-brown leather with raw, irregular edges. A dense fringe of thick, twisted brown rope tassels hangs dynamically along the hips, interspersed with small tufts of tan fur. The entire assembly is secured by wrapped, thick corded ropes around the waist.Matching accessories include wide, rigid bracers made of thick, unadorned black leather wrapped securely around both forearms.
