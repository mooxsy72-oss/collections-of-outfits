A zippered utility pocket sits on the left upper sleeve.Beneath the jacket is a black ribbed knit turtleneck sweater with extended cuffs that protrude past the jacket sleeves. Wrapped over the neck is a long, heavy black knit scarf that hangs vertically down the torso.The trousers are slim-fit black tactical cargo pants made of lightweight, low-drape synthetic fabric. They feature flat utility pockets on the outer thighs and taper cleanly toward the ankles.Footwear consists of high-top black leather combat boots.
