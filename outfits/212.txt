OUTFIT: outfit is a fantasy-rococo inspired, highly volumetric high-low dress ensemble featuring an hourglass silhouette with an exaggerated lower volume, a structured bodice, and multi-layered fabric distribution. The color scheme is predominantly soft pink with white lace accents.The bodice is a structured, skin-tight corset that extends from a low sweet-heart neckline down to a pointed V-shape at the high hip. It features vertical boning lines, a central lace-up closure system, and an integrated under-bust lift. Off-the-shoulder straps form short, highly puffed, ruffled sleeves that end at the upper bicep, exposing the shoulders and chest.The attached skirt features an asymmetrical high-low hemline. The front section is mini-length, ending at the upper thigh with dense, multi-layered tiers of gathered ruffles that flare outward. The back section cascades into a floor-length train that pools on the ground. The immense volume and flare of the skirt are achieved through extensive fabric gathering, layered tulle, and stiffened ruffles rather than internal hoop structures. White floral lace trims adorn every hem, tier boundary, and seam line across the skirt and bodice.A matching fabric choker with dimensional pink fabric roses is worn around the mid-neck. The outfit concludes with a pair of solid white, smooth-textured high-heeled pumps with a pointed toe box, which are visible from the front beneath the short hemline.
