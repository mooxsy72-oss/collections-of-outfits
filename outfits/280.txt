The upper bodice line is trimmed with a thick, horizontal band of smooth, opaque white fabric. Centered on this band is an oversized, prominent white fabric bow with two long, wide vertical tails extending down past the waistline. The bodice is closely fitted and tailored through the torso down to the natural waist, crafted from a thick, heavy crimson-red velvet fabric that has a plush, matte texture. This red velvet is subtly embellished with small, sparse, scattered sparkling rhinestones or glitter dots across its entire surface. The velvet fabric possesses significant thickness and structure, providing a smooth, rigid shape retention that defines the fitted torso.The skirt of the dress starts at the natural waist and flares out into an exaggerated, wide bell-like volume, dominating the lower silhouette. This outer velvet skirt layer features deep, structured folds and a stiff drape that holds its wide shape away from the body. Underneath this crimson velvet layer is a secondary, voluminous white petticoat layer made of a lighter weight, heavily gathered fabric. This under-layer features a dense, ruffled hemline that extends slightly lower than the red velvet skirt, creating a visible, fluffy white decorative border around the thighs. The extreme brevity of the double-layered skirt exposes the entire length of the legs from the mid-thigh down.The footwear consists of a pair of pointed-toe pumps with high, slender stiletto heels. The shoes are entirely covered in a dense, heavily textured coating of multi-toned metallic glitter, reflecting light with a high-shine sparkle that contrasts with the matte velvet of the dress. The pumps feature a low-cut vamp that exposes the top of the foot.
