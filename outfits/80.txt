OUTFIT: an outfit blends European theatrical traditions, combining elements of a classic Renaissance pierrot or court jester with celestial fantasy. The silhouette is dominated by an exaggerated, multi-layered white ruff collar, a voluminous star-shaped headpiece, and a structured, open-front tulle overskirt.The main garment is a form-fitting, long-sleeved velvet bodysuit in a golden-mustard hue, providing full arm and torso coverage. Over the bodysuit sits a stiff, heavily structured underbust corset made of textured, vertically paneled brocade fabric in a matching ochre shade, cinching the waist. The high, dramatic ruff collar frames the neck with dense, crisp pleats and a gold-trimmed edge. Flared white ruffles with matching gold trim accentuate the wrists.A sheer, lightweight white tulle overskirt cascades from the rear waist to the calves. It features horizontal structural bands, gathered volume, and small daisy appliques spaced along the trim. Opaque white tights cover the legs entirely. The footwear consists of metallic gold boots finished with voluminous, pleated ankle ruffs matching the collar. A prominent celestial headpiece features a central crescent moon face flanked by five stuffed, textured orange-gold points. All fabric materials range from stiff, shape-retaining structured elements to plush, light-absorbing velvet.
