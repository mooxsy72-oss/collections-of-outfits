OUTFIT: outfit belongs to a dark fantasy and gothic warrior clothing tradition, heavily inspired by insectoid, demonic, and armored aesthetics. The style is supported by the sharp, skeletal metallic plates, segmented armor lines, long clawed gauntlets, and horn-like protrusions on the mask. The overall visual composition is dark, monochromatic, and highly structured, relying on a dramatic contrast between deep black textures and reflective, polished silver armor. It features an exaggerated, dramatic hourglass silhouette with immense volume radiating outward from the hips and shoulders, balancing the extremely cinched, armored torso and drawing immediate visual attention to the sharp metallic lines of the lower legs and elongated claws.The upper body begins with a highly structured, metallic silver corset bodice adorned with overlapping, scalloped plate details that mimic insect scales or stylized organic armor. It features a sweetheart neckline, tight waist construction, and integrates seamlessly with a sheer black tulle high-neck collar that transitions into ruffled, voluminous cap sleeves. A dark, sheer black textile underlayer covers the torso, showing open panels at the chest and hips. For the lower body, the outfit utilizes tight-fitting black fabric leggings or hosiery beneath form-fitting, highly reflective silver plate armor that encases the shins and knees with segmented, mechanical joint lines and pointed knee caps.The dramatic outerwear consists of a massive, billowing full-length skirt cape made from multiple layers of lightweight, sheer black tulle and mesh fabric. This cape gathers heavily at the back waist, draping into a voluminous, floor-sweeping train that billows outward to create a cloud-like, dark silhouette behind the legs. On the feet are high-heeled black leather boots with a sharp stiletto heel and thin front laces.The accessories are visually dominant and central to the theme. Striking clawed gauntlets fit over the forearms in a segmented black and silver design, extending past the fingers into five long, menacingly curved, needle-sharp metallic silver talons. A matching full-face masquerade mask covers the head, constructed from polished silver filigree and ornate plating. It features sharp, organic textures around the eye cutouts and boasts two prominent, sleek black horns that curve sharply upward and slightly outward from the forehead, ending in tapered points.
