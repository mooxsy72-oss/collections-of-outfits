OUTFIT: This floor-length medieval fantasy gown features an empire waist silhouette defined by a high-contrast black and white palette. The garment incorporates stylistic elements reminiscent of high-fantasy and romanticized historical dress, evidenced by its ultra-long, floor-sweeping hanging sleeves and structured metallic trims.The gown is constructed from lightweight, semi-sheer chiffon. Black chiffon forms the outer panels of the fluid, loose-fitting skirt and the dramatic sleeves. The central vertical column of the gown is made from a gathered white chiffon fabric, which provides a crinkled, highly textured contrast to the smooth drape of the sides.An off-the-shoulder neckline is framed by a thick, dark metallic gold band with an openwork, braided wire texture. A matching broad decorative girdle wraps tightly around the empire waist. This waist belt is detailed with horizontal cording and symmetrical gold metal clasps or frog-style loop closures across the center. The sleeves are tightly gathered at the top of the arms before opening into expansive, unlined fabric panels that cascade entirely to the floor.
