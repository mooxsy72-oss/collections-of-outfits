OUTFIT: costume belongs to a contemporary fantasy and theatrical runway tradition, drawing heavily on angelic iconography through its use of oversized wings and elaborate, gemstone-encrusted body ornamentation. The palette is strictly monochromatic, dominated by brilliant white and accented by reflective silver metallic and clear crystal tones.The only traditional garment is a pair of high-waisted, floor-length white satin trousers. They feature a relaxed, fluid straight-leg cut that flows smoothly with high shape retention, leaving the entire torso, back, and arms uncovered with excess tissue.The silhouette is dominated by massive, exaggerated accessories that provide extreme visual volume. A pair of large, structured white angel wings attaches to the back, densely layered with overlapping faux feathers. The wings are heavily embellished with integrated white fabric roses, draped strands of white pearls, and large, teardrop-cut crystals.An intricate, rigid body armor harness sits across the upper chest and shoulders. This epaulet-style piece is encrusted with a dense mosaic of silver rhinestones, large clear faux gems, and multiple tiers of cascading pearl strands that drape over the upper arms. A matching, wide cummerbund-style waist cincher wraps the trouser waistline, mirroring the same heavy rhinestone and pearl embellishments. A thick, multi-strand rhinestone bracelet coordinates on the wrist.
