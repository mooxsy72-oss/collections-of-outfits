OUTFIT: romantic, winter-inspired outfit utilizes a balanced, high-contrast color palette of creamy off-white and deep burgundy red. The color distribution alternates evenly from head to toe, creating a coordinated look where the structured, textured bodice and the rich velvet textures draw the most visual attention.The upper body begins with a strapless, heavily structured corset bodice in a cream tone, featuring a curved sweetheart neckline, vertical boning channels, and a pointed, dip-down front hem. It is made from a thick, rigid jacquard or brocade fabric with an all-over raised floral damask pattern. Underneath the corset, delicate off-white lace fingerless gloves wrap around the hands and forearms. For the lower body, a short, fitted burgundy velvet mini-skirt sits low on the waist, featuring a straight hemline and a layered wrap-around or tiered front flap. Layered underneath are sheer, white patterned tights adorned with a dense, repeating lace vine or bow motif.The outerwear consists of a mid-calf length, open-front trench coat in a solid, bright off-white shade. Crafted from a thick, heavy wool blend fabric, it features oversized notched lapels, long relaxed sleeves, and a soft drape. On the feet are knee-high, deep burgundy platform boots with a highly reflective, glossy patent leather finish, chunky platform soles, and tall stiletto heels.Accessories include a soft, rounded burgundy wool beret resting on the head. A matching burgundy velvet ribbon choker is tied tightly around the neck, finished with a long, trailing bow at the front.
