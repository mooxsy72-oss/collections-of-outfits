OUTFIT: a post-apocalyptic survivor ensemble utilizes utilitarian garments and tactical leather hardware. The dominant colors are olive green, denim blue, and various shades of weathered brown, devoid of patterns.The silhouette is dominated by an oversized, heavy olive-green winter tailored parka. This thick, padded jacket features a large hood trimmed with voluminous, fluffy brown faux fur, long sleeves with gathered cuffs, a front zipper closure with large button details, and multiple cargo pockets. Contrasting the bulky upper body, secondary slim-fitting blue denim jeans feature a mid-rise waist, standard five-pocket styling, and a straight-leg cut that stacks slightly at the bottom.Multiple structured leather accessories add tactical volume. A prominent, asymmetrical utility rig wraps denim thighs with crisscrossing dark brown leather belts, hosting attached pouches, a hanging metallic key, and a pocket watch. Weathered brown leather fingerless gloves cover the hands, detailed with multiple buckled wrist straps.
