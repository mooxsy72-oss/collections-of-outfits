OUTFIT: all-black techwear and streetwear ensemble relies on heavy layering, oversized volumes, and utility details. The dominant silhouette is defined by an oversized, bulky bomber jacket made from thick, structured nylon or polyester with strong shape retention, wide sleeves, gathered cuffs, and a utility zipper pocket on the left bicep. The jacket features a prominent bright orange lining visible along the front zipper opening. Beneath, a loose, relaxed-fit black crewneck t-shirt provides a soft jersey knit drape, showing a hint of a striped collar trim at the neckline. On the lower half, slim-fit, highly structured black cargo pants feature multiple oversized utility pockets, straps, and buckles that add significant visual texture and weight. The pants taper sharply at the lower leg, where they are tucked into matte black leather combat boots. These high-top boots have thick, rugged rubber soles and a secure lace-up construction. Wearable accessories include chunky, reflective silver metallic chain bracelets on both wrists and simple dark metallic rings on the fingers, adding stark focal points against the monochromatic black. The entire outfit fully covers the body, emphasizing exaggerated upper-body volume contrasting with a structured, tapered lower half.
