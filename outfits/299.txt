OUTFIT: outfit presents a contemporary, deconstructed interpretation of prep school uniforms mixed with modern tailoring. The dominant color is muted brown, accented by stark white and deep black. Visual focus is drawn to the open, bare midriff created by the cropped nature of the upper layer, contrasting with the structured, traditional lines of the tailored components.The base layer consists of a classic, crisp white cotton button-down dress shirt with a stiff, pointed collar. It is worn fully closed to the throat. Layered over this is a cropped blazer jacket made from a heavy, structured woven wool fabric featuring a small-scale, dark brown and tan glen plaid pattern. The blazer has a boxy silhouette with padded shoulders, wide notched lapels, and long, straight sleeves. The hem is cut squarely across the ribs, completely exposing the waist, and is held together across the front by a single, large silver metallic safety pin.The lower-body garment is a matching mid-rise miniskirt in the identical dark brown and tan glen plaid suiting fabric. It features a straight, slightly wrap-style silhouette that secures on the left hip with a large dark button. The legs are covered in sheer, ultra-fine black hosiery.Footwear consists of high-gloss, black patent leather platform oxford shoes. They feature a chunky, rugged rubber lug sole, a rounded toe box, and thin black laces laced through the front eyelets.Accessories include a solid black, matte necktie knotted tightly under the white collar. A quilted black leather flap bag hangs from a silver-toned metal interwoven chain strap, held in the hand.
