OUTFIT: dark-academic ensemble relies on a monochrome color scheme dominated by black, with stark white and silver accents creating strong visual contrast. The outfit leans heavily on structured, tailored geometry, drawing immediate attention to the cinched pinstripe blazer dress and the towering, heavy-soled boots.The base layer consists of a crisp white, plain-woven cotton dress shirt. It features a stiff, pointed collar tightly fastened at the throat and long, fitted sleeves that extend slightly past the wrists to show smooth white cuffs. Layered over this is a black-and-white pinstripe blazer worn as a mini dress. The fabric is a mid-weight, highly structured suiting material with fine, vertical white stripes spaced evenly across a solid black ground. It is designed with padded, pronounced shoulders, long tailored sleeves, and wide, peaked lapels. The double-breasted jacket has a straight hemline ending at the upper thigh, with horizontal flap pockets positioned symmetrically on the lower hips.Sheer, matte black nylon hosiery covers the legs completely from the thigh down. The footwear features knee-high, black leather platform boots with a highly structured, rigid silhouette. These boots are defined by thick, chunky block heels, exaggerated platform soles, and a front lace-up closure system that extends from the toe to the top shaft.Accessories include a narrow, solid black silk necktie knotted tightly under the shirt collar. A wide, smooth black leather belt with a rectangular, matte-black interlocking buckle is cinched tightly around the natural waist of the blazer. Finally, a small, structured black leather handbag with a curved top handle and a fold-over front flap is carried at the side.
