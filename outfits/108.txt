OUTFIT: This outfit is composed of earthy tones distributed across layered, tailoring-inspired pieces with an exaggerated, voluminous silhouette. Brown, beige, and cream are the dominant colors, accented by a gold-toned accessory. The main visual anchors are the massive, floor-length outer coat and a sharp, patterned three-piece suit.The outermost layer is a floor-length, open-front overcoat made from thick, heavy, high-pile faux fur in a mixed tan and beige color. It features an oversized, relaxed silhouette with dropped shoulders and wide, tubular sleeves that completely cover the arms down to the wrists. A wide, substantial notched collar frames the open neckline, showing off the underlayers without visible closures or hardware along the straight hem.Beneath the coat is a tailored, mid-weight wool three-piece suit showcasing a classic brown and beige glen check plaid pattern. The double-breasted suit jacket has structured, padded shoulders, wide peak lapels, and a straight hem ending past the hips. The matching vest buttons high on the chest, leaving room for a crisp, stiff-collared white cotton dress shirt. The matching wide-leg trousers have a relaxed, flowing drape that pools slightly over dark, heavy-soled dress shoes.A gold silk necktie with a dense, small-scale floral or paisley pattern adds a decorative pop of color against the shirt. The ensemble is accessorized with dark, rectangular-framed sunglasses featuring black lenses.
