OUTFIT: fantasy attire draws from the historical-fantasy witch tradition, defined by its stylized, high-volume layers and dramatic silhouettes. The visual composition relies on a bold, high-contrast palette of deep charcoal-grey and vibrant terracotta-red. Color distribution alternates cleanly across the layers, which lack any patterns. Visual attention is anchored by the exaggeratedly oversized pointed hat and the sheer volume of the cascading outerwear drapes.An upper-body terracotta-red tunic or vest features a structured, high funnel collar framing the neck. Below it sits a dark charcoal-grey full-body robe or tunic that extends past the knees. This mid-calf garment is highly structured with angular, pointed panel hems that create a jagged silhouette. It is tightly cinched at the waist by a wide, matching dark sash. Outerwear consists of multiple heavily layered pieces. A short, structured charcoal-grey capelet wraps the upper shoulders, ending in sharp, pointed geometric edges. Beneath it, a secondary, oversized terracotta-red wrap or cloak drapes loosely over the arms, falling in voluminous folds that extend down to the mid-calf.Footwear consists of fitted, knee-high dark charcoal boots with a soft, creased fabric texture. They feature contrasting terracotta-red laces running up the front and a pointed toe structure. For accessories, a massive, structured fabric witch's hat dominates the upper silhouette. It features an exceptionally wide, upturned terracotta-red brim lined with a dark charcoal-grey upper, topped with a tall, rumpled, and twisted crown that curls at the peak and is accented by a thin gold-embroidered trim around the band. Dark, fitted leather gloves finish the look.
