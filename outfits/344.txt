Visual attention is drawn primarily to the intricate, form-fitting lace pattern across the torso and legs, framed by the dramatically bulky, oversized forearm sleeves.The primary garment is a full-body, skintight lace bodysuit ensemble. The upper section features a structured corset bodice with molded sweetheart cups, a defined waist construction, and slim halter-style straps looping behind the neck. It provides low bust coverage and transitions seamlessly into the lower body, extending down the legs as full-length, form-fitting leggings. They feature a sharp pointed toe box, a high stiletto heel, and a snug fit through the calves before flaring slightly above the knees with a clean, straight hem.Wearable accessories are highlighted by a pair of dramatically oversized, bulky forearm sleeves made from long-pile, shaggy black faux fur that extends from the mid-forearm over the wrists.
