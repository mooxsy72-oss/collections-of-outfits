OUTFIT: tribal fantasy barbarian outfit relies on asymmetrical, primal layering and macabre trophies to build its aggressive silhouette. The dominant colors are monochromatic earth tones, featuring charcoal-gray, muddy brown, and a contrasting bone-white. The most visually dominant elements are the animal skulls mounted at the waist and the shaggy, asymmetrical fur shoulder pad.The upper torso is largely bare, defined by an asymmetrical dark brown leather harness that crosses from the right waist to the left shoulder. Atop the left shoulder, a bulky patch of shaggy, coarse brown animal fur adds localized volume. Both forearms are tightly wrapped from the wrists to the mid-bicep in textured, charcoal-gray fabric bands, leaving the hands and upper shoulders exposed.At the waist, a thick dark brown leather belt secures a highly tattered, medium-weight loincloth or fauld skirt made of charcoal-gray fabric. This skirt features an irregular, jagged hem with multiple long, hanging fabric shreds. Mounted directly to the front of this belt are two prominent, weathered bone-white animal skulls—one centrally placed horned skull and a smaller skull on the left hip—which create a bulky, structured focal point. Beneath the skirt, form-fitting, dark brown leather or heavy cloth trousers feature horizontal ribbed segmentations along the thighs. The outfit finishes with mid-calf dark brown leather boots, trimmed at the top cuffs with a thick layer of coarse brown fur, mirroring the texture of the shoulder piece.
