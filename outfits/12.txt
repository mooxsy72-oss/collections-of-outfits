OUTFIT: The traditional ensemble consists of a loose linen blouse tucked into a high-waisted, voluminous midi-length skirt. The cream-white blouse features a gathered, drawstring neckline accented by a central split placket with dark red tassels, and billowy bishop sleeves cinched at the forearms. Intricate cross-stitch embroidery depicting dark red and black floral grape clusters decorates the shoulders, upper chest, and upper sleeves of the blouse. The solid-colored, medium-saturation emerald green skirt is tightly gathered at the waist, flaring into a full A-line silhouette that ends above the ankles. Two horizontal bands of shiny, dark pink velvet ribbon trim are stitched parallel to each other near the skirt's hem. A wide, woven textile sash in a repeating geometric pattern of red, black, and beige stripes wraps securely around the waist, with two long fringed ends hanging down the front. A short, chunky necklace made of rounded, vibrant red beads wraps around the neck. Neat brown leather boots complete the look.
