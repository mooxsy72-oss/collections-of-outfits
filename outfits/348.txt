OUTFIT: outfit belongs to a modern Western fashion tradition, blending preppy subculture elements like tartan plaid with contemporary winter streetwear textures. This classification is supported by the combination of a coordinated schoolgirl-style pleated skirt and corset top paired with cozy faux-fur outerwear. The overall visual composition is dominated by a deep burgundy-red hue, which serves as both a solid block color for the outerwear and accessories, and the base for the multi-colored plaid pattern. A medium-scale tartan plaid pattern consisting of red, black, and beige intersecting lines decorates the core layers, drawing immediate visual attention alongside the heavily textured, tiered fringe elements on the legs.The upper-body garment is a form-fitting, strapless corset top that offers low chest coverage with its straight across, subtly curved neckline. It is made from a structured, mid-weight woven fabric that holds a rigid shape, finished at the top edge with a delicate black lace trim and a tiny red ribbon bow at the center bust. The lower-body garment is a matching low-rise tartan pleated mini-skirt. It features a wide, asymmetrical cross-over waistband that dips slightly at the center, releasing into crisp, sharp box pleats that flare outward to create structured volume at the hips, terminating in a straight hemline above the mid-thigh.The outerwear layer consists of a bulky, oversized cropped jacket made from thick, plush burgundy faux fur. It features an open-front construction, dropped shoulders, long relaxed sleeves, and an exaggerated, wide notched lapel collar that adds significant visual weight to the upper body. Footwear features dramatic, over-the-knee boots crafted from a matte burgundy suede or fabric. The shaft of each boot is entirely covered by three tiers of long, densely layered fringe that sways vertically, ending in a sharp pointed toe box.
