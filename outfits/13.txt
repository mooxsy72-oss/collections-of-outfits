OUTFIT: The traditional ensemble consists of a loose-fitting blouse tucked into a high-waisted, voluminous maxi skirt. The matte, cream-white fabric blouse features a gathered, crew-style neckline and billowy bishop sleeves that cinch into tight, embroidered cuffs. Intricate geometric cross-stitch embroidery in shades of dark red, black, and orange forms dense rectangular panels across the shoulders and lower forearms. The solid-colored, matte not bright dark blue skirt is constructed with structured knife pleats at the waistline, falling into a full, floor-sweeping A-line silhouette. Two horizontal bands of solid red ribbon trim border a panel of orange and white embroidered geometric diamond motifs located just above the skirt's hem. A wide, textured red textile sash wraps around the high waist, with two long knit ends featuring large pom-poms hanging down the front center. Neat dark red leather boots complete the look.
