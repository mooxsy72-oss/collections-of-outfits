OUTFIT: fantasy East-Asian fusion ensemble utilizes a stark split-color distribution, blending cream-white and jet-black as co-dominant shades, heavily accented by dense, metallic-gold rococo baroque swirls. The silhouette features intentionally extreme, boxy padding across the upper torso, forming sharp, exaggerated ninety-degree angles at the shoulders that contrast with the fluid, fluidly-draping fabrics below.The outfit consists of a wrap-front inner robe and a floor-length outer coat. The inner robe is constructed from mid-weight, high-shine cream silk satin, plunging into a deep, overlapping V-neckline that exposes the upper chest and collarbones. It is bound at the waist by a wide, self-fabric sash that loops into a long trailing tie on the left side. Over this sits a structured outer coat made of fluid silk, featuring a split-color design: jet-black on the right side and cream-white on the left. The coat is defined by massive, voluminous bell sleeves that pool heavily at the forearms. Ornate gold filigree embroidery details the outer sleeve curves, shoulders, and lower hems, forming intricate borders. Wearable accessories include a wide, multi-strand diamond choker collar and matching teardrop diamond earrings.
