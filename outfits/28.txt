OUTFIT: An avant-garde ensemble references historical 18th-century Baroque and Rococo aesthetics through an exaggerated, dramatic silhouette. The overall color scheme is predominantly cream/off-white with contrasting black borders, lines, and metallic gold accents. A structured, strapless sweetheart-neckline corset fits tightly around the torso, exposing the upper chest, shoulders, and arms. It features vertical paneling constructed from stiff, medium-weight woven fabric with a fine tweed-like texture. A prominent black cord laces up the center front through metallic grommets. The top and bottom edges are trimmed with scalloped black contrast borders, and the vertical structural seams are defined by matching black lines.Exaggerated, voluminous gigot-style balloon sleeves sit detached around the upper arms. They feature highly gathered, structured cream fabric that maintains massive round volume, ending in structured, wavy ruffle flounces edged with thin black trim.The voluminous overskirt features a dramatic high-low silhouette, exposing the legs fully at the front while cascading into a wide, pooling train at the back. It is constructed from heavy cream fabric with a stiff drape, forming deep, structured waves and folds. The perimeter is finished with a continuous, wavy ruffle trim outlined in black.Thigh-high boots feature a sleek, form-fitting black material, a very high platform, block heels, and a front-lace design.A heavy, ornate filigree statement necklace in antiqued gold sits high on the collarbone, featuring intricate baroque relief work. Multiple wide, Chunky gold filigree cuff bracelets and stacked metallic bangles adorn both wrists.
