OUTFIT: outfit combines elements of dark fantasy armor and ecclesiastical vestments, using a dramatic contrast between highly structured metallic spikes and fluid, heavily draped textiles. The color palette is strictly limited to metallic silver-grey and deep matte black, punctuated by intricate silver embroidery. Visual attention is split equally between the highly dangerous-looking, spiked upper-body armor and the religious iconography of the central tunic.At the core is a long, floor-length black velvet gown featuring a continuous, dense floral damask pattern. Layered over this is a long, sleeveless black chasuble-style vestment that hangs straight down to the shins with a squared hem. This vest is densely embellished with silver embroidered cross motifs along the borders and an ornate, symmetrical baroque floral panel at the bottom center. A series of metallic rosary-style chains hangs across the chest, dangling three distinct silver crucifixes down the front.The upper torso, shoulders, and arms are encased in rigid, heavy-weight steel plate armor with a weathered, matte finish. The pauldrons are massively oversized, rounded, and dense with sharp, radially projecting spikes. Matching spiked gauntlets and vambraces completely cover the arms, adding immense upper-body volume. A structured fabric veil hangs from beneath a crowned helmet, completely obscuring the head. The helmet itself features an articulated visor and a circular crown of vertical steel spikes, reinforcing the aggressive, imposing silhouette.
