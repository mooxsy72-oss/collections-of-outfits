OUTFIT: ensemble heavily draws from Coquette, Japanese Lolita, and historical Rococo-undergarment fantasy aesthetics. The outfit features an ultra-short, multi-layered lace romper or mini skirt set paired with a heavily structured corset bodice, presenting an intentional silhouette of exaggerated hip volume contrasted with a tightly cinched waist.The main garment consists of a sleeveless, bone-structured white lace corset bodice with sheer lace paneling across the ribs, satin underwire cups, and thin lace shoulder straps. Faux-pearl strands drape across the chest from the neckline. Suspender garter straps hang from the lower hemline.Separate, matching white lace garter-style bands wrap around the upper arms, featuring gathered trim, dangling satin ribbons, and hanging pearl teardrops. The neck is adorned with a layered choker accessory made of wire, white pearls, and a central metallic pendant. The color palette is monochromatic stark white with a dominant texture of intricate floral lace overlays, sheer mesh, and smooth satin bindings.
