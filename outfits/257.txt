OUTFIT: fantasy costume relies heavily on textures, evoking a mermaid or siren aesthetic. It is a highly revealing two-piece set consisting of a heavily embellished bralette and a low-slung, multi-textured micro mini skirt. The palette is monochromatic silver, shimmering iridescent white, and clear crystal accents.The top is a skimpy, cropped bandeau-style bralette. It features a straight neckline supported by two thin silver chain shoulder straps. The fabric is a dense, stiff mesh completely encrusted with clear glass beads, pearls, and iridescent sequins. Hanging from the lower hem is a prominent fringe of dangling crystal strands of varying lengths, ending in tiny teardrop beads.The low-rise mini skirt sits on the hips, leaving the entire midriff and upper thighs exposed. It features a multi-layered construction with a metallic silver knit base, overlaid with a draped, loosely woven net panel decorated with small cream floral embroidery. A thick, bulky silver-metallic rope cord is knotted dramatically on the right hip, cascading downward with long, heavy fringe.Accessories include a matching multi-strand crystal body chain that loops around the neck and connects down to the hips, alongside two identical beaded fringe wrist cuffs.
