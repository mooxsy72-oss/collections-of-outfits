OUTFIT: ensemble blends 18th-century Rococo fashion with a modern lingerie-inspired silhouette. The entire color distribution is monochromatic ivory and cream, with no secondary colors or high-contrast patterns.The primary piece is a heavily structured, strapless corset bodice with a straight neckline, exposing the shoulders, collarbones, and chest. It is constructed from stiff satin fabric with vertical boning, a central row of small satin bows, and a lace-trimmed top edge. Detached, sheer white lace bishop sleeves cover the arms, gathering tightly into ruffled cuffs. Below the cinched waist, a highly structured, skeletal pannier frame replaces a traditional skirt. This ultra-short, caged crinoline features three horizontal boned tiers wrapped in densely gathered lace ruffles, completely exposing the thighs and midriff area beneath.Thigh-high semi-sheer white stockings cover the legs, secured with voluminous, elasticized ivory lace garters decorated with centered fabric bows. On the feet are open-toe white satin pumps embellished with a pearl-encrusted ankle strap and a pearl-beaded bow over the toes. Headwear consists of a large, rigid ivory satin bow attached to a headband, supporting three towering, wispy cream ostrich feathers. A tiered white pearl choker wraps the neck, and an intricate, fan-shaped white lace folding fan is held as a focal accessory.
