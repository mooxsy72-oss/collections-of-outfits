OUTFIT: a fantasy-style costume evokes a gothic, dark-forest sorceress aesthetic supported by a dramatic antler headdress and a somber, monochromatic color palette. The entire outfit is solid black, utilizing contrasting matte and sheer textures rather than visual patterns.A structured antler headdress serves as the dominant visual focal point, featuring large, naturally curved, multi-pointed deer or elk antlers attached to a dark headband base. Draped over this headdress and cascading down the upper back is a secondary, lightweight sheer black lace veil with an intricate, scalloped floral embroidery border.The main garment consists of a two-piece or paneled gown layering. The upper torso features a form-fitting, long-sleeved bodice made from sheer black floral lace fabric, tightly following the figure with a high crew neckline and continuous slim sleeves down to the wrists. Below the natural waistline, the silhouette transitions into a secondary, highly voluminous A-line maxi skirt. This full, floor-length skirt is constructed from layers of lightweight, fine black tulle or mesh fabric that drapes heavily to the ground, pooling over the forest floor to create a subtle train.
