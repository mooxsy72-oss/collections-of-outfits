OUTFIT: The ensemble draws on the traditions of the Coquette and Sweet Lolita subcultures, characterized by historical romanticism, abundant frills, and decorative bows. The color palette is built on a combination of soft pink, cream, and white with small floral prints. A fitted cropped corset top with a gathered sweetheart neckline and thin straps is complemented by a multitude of puffy white and pink ruffles. Separate detachable sleeves of translucent lace flare below the wrists into voluminous ruffles. An asymmetrical low-rise miniskirt consists of tiered cascades of white lace and pink ruffles, creating strong visual volume and draping at the sides. A wide pink sash is tied at the hip in a large rose pattern. The lower part of the silhouette is deliberately weighted with very voluminous, boldly accentuated knitted cream-colored leg warmers. Their pronounced horizontal gathers significantly increase the proportions of the shins. The tops of the socks are decorated with pink satin bows. The look is completed by chunky platform sneakers in pink and white tones. A pink bow is pinned to the head.
