OUTFIT: a fantasy cottagecore-meets-steampunk mini dress features a corseted, high-low silhouette blending rustic textiles. The dress belongs to a whimsical fantasy tradition, supported by its exaggerated ruffled textures, layered high-low skirt, and antique sheet music motif.The dominant colors are faded denim blue and antique cream, with secondary accents of muted orange, purple, and crimson red.The bodice is a tightly fitted, structured corset with a deep, sweetheart neckline and a defined natural waist. Its central panel features a medium-scale antique sheet music print overlaid with dense, warm-toned floral motifs. Flanking this central panel is a dark denim fabric that extends into oversized, voluminous cap sleeves with heavily gathered, flared ruffles.The skirt dominates the silhouette through extreme volume and layering. The outermost layer consists of a heavy, structured denim fabric split down the center, draped in dramatic, asymmetrical tiers that are shorter in the front and longer in the back. Beneath the denim, an underskirt of lightweight, fluidly draping cream linen cascades in soft, irregular gathers. A dense cluster of frayed, multi-colored fabric ruffles in crimson and purple accents the front waist, bridging the bodice and the highly voluminous, multi-layered skirt.
