OUTFIT: a traditional slavic folk costume consisting of a full-length, A-line red sarafan dress over a loose white chemise blouse. The red puffy pinafore dress features an ankle-length hemline, a high square neckline with thick shoulder straps, and a solid-colored, moderately saturated matte fabric. A decorative woven border with geometric patterns in gold (predominant), black, and red trims the vertical center seam and the bottom hemline of the dress. The long-sleeved white linen chemise blouse has voluminous, gathered bishop sleeves tapering into narrow wrist cuffs, adorned with red and orange embroidered geometric bands across the upper arms. On her head, a patterned brown, gold, and cream floral-motif kerchief scarf is tied snugly beneath her chin. traditional woven bast shoes made of light tan, textured plant fiber, worn over thick cream-colored fabric footwraps crisscrossed with matching fiber ties around the ankles.
