OUTFIT: fantasy academic wizard ensemble presents a structured, modest look dominated by deep black and charcoal tones, with a secondary pop of forest green adding a tactile focal point to the upper torso. Visual focus is split between the thick, highly textured knit patterning of the sweater and the voluminous, fluid drape of the open floor-length robe.The primary upper-body garment is a thick, heavyweight forest green sweater featuring a dense, oversized cable-knit pattern running vertically across the entire torso. It has a high, close-fitting crew neck and straight long sleeves finished with a wide, ribbed knit cuff in a slightly lighter brownish-khaki shade that matches the ribbed bottom hem. Layered below is a floor-length, high-waisted black maxi skirt constructed from a medium-weight woven fabric with full, crisp pleats that extend to the ankles, creating an A-line silhouette with moderate shape retention.Serving as outerwear, an oversized, voluminous black wizard robe hangs loosely from the shoulders down to the floor, presenting an open-front construction that fully showcases the inner layers. The robe is crafted from a fluid, dense fabric that drapes heavily, featuring deeply set shoulders and wide, bell-shaped sleeves that pool slightly at the forearms. For footwear, polished dark brown oxford-style shoes feature a low block heel, thick structured soles, and clean toe cap stitching. Dark grey or black matte leather gloves cover the hands completely, tapering neatly at the wrists beneath the sweater cuffs.
