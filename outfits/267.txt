OUTFIT: contemporary, stylized sharp formal suit ensemble features a dominant chocolate-brown color accented by a secondary muted slate-gray shirt and black and silver metallic hardware. The look presents an elongated, tailored silhouette with an open chest area.The tailored brown blazer has sharply structured, padded shoulders, notched lapels, and long, slim sleeves. It is made of smooth, medium-weight fabric with high shape retention and a subtle sheen. Underneath, a lightweight, fluidly draping slate-gray dress shirt is completely unbuttoned down to the mid-torso, exposing the entire chest and collarbones. The shirt features a soft collar and a visible front button placket. The matching brown trousers are high-waisted, slim-fit, and feature a sharp center crease along the legs, dropping straight down to cover the feet.A black leather belt with a prominent silver graphic "S" buckle secures the waist. Decorative accessories include a layered metallic neck chain with a small square pendant and an ornate silver flower brooch pinned to the left lapel, which connects to a long, delicate silver chain draping across the jacket's chest.
