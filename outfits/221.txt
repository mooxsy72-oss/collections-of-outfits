OUTFIT: monochromatic black streetwear outfit features an intentionally oversized, heavy silhouette that maximizes upper-body volume through deep layering and bulky fabrics. The dominant color is solid black, while the footwear provides a striking contrast in bright white and metallic silver.The outermost layer is an exaggerated, voluminous bomber jacket in solid black leather or heavy faux-leather. It features an extremely puffy, padded structure that creates a rounded, barrel-like silhouette. The jacket incorporates a flat shirt collar, dropped shoulders, very wide sleeves, and thick, horizontally ribbed elastic at the cuffs and hemline. Underneath sits a dense black cotton-fleece hoodie; its large, structured hood is pulled up over the head, completely obscuring the neckline. A black knit beanie or skullcap is worn tight to the head beneath the hood, leaving only the lower face exposed.The bottom half consists of relaxed, wide-leg black trousers or sweatpants that drape loosely down the legs with minimal tapering. They gather in small, heavy folds around the ankles, ensuring full lower-body coverage. The look is grounded by bulky, retro-style running sneakers featuring a bright white mesh base, chunky white soles, and prominent metallic silver geometric overlays.
