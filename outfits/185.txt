OUTFIT: fantasy ensemble adapts elements of 18th-century European court dress into a stylized, avant-garde silhouette. The historical influence is defined by an integrated pannier cage structure and structural bodice, subverted by exposing the legs completely at the front. The dominant color is a soft dusty rose pink, with crisp white acting as a secondary color for the soft inner layers.The base layer features a white, lightweight, sheer chiffon chemise with a gathered scoop neckline and voluminous, billowy multi-tiered puff sleeves that cinch above the elbows. Over this sits a structured, form-fitting dusty rose corset-style bodice with shoulder straps, a square neckline, a pointed front waist, and an embroidered glittery bow detail on the center chest.At the hips, a short, heavily gathered white tulle petticoat flares straight out, under which a rigid, metallic gold wire pannier frame extends horizontally. This cage structure is embellished with intricate geometric lattice patterns and a dense fringe of long, dangling crystal glass beads that shimmer. From the back of the waist, a heavy, floor-length dusty rose train cascades downward, heavily embellished along its vertical borders with dense, textured pink and silver metallic floral embroidery.The footwear consists of pointed-toe pumps encrusted with matching pink glitter. A delicate, multi-tiered gold chain choker accents the neck.
