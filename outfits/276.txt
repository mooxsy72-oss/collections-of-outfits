OUTFIT: high-fashion avant-garde two-piece set features a heavily cropped structured jacket and high-waisted shorts made from stiff, shape-retaining, heavy-weight metallic silver glitter fabric. The silhouette is dominated by an asymmetric top with extreme, exaggerated proportions that leave the midriff, collarbones, and chest exposed. The jacket’s right side features an oversized, sharply padded structured shoulder with a full-length, straight sleeve ending in silver metallic finger cots. The left side transitions into a sheer, lightweight mesh sleeve adorned with vertical crystal fringe lines, topped by a large, rigid 3D sculptural butterfly wing that extends dramatically upward past the shoulder. Underneath, a built-in, highly structured corset-style bralette forms a deeply plunging neckline. It is entirely constructed from dense, metallic silver wire and crystal embroidery shaped like an intricate, symmetrical butterfly. The high-waisted shorts have a tailored, structured fit with a wide, self-fabric belt and a chunky silver buckle. They sit high on the natural waist and flare slightly at the mid-thigh, offering structured volume that balances the dramatic upper silhouette. The uniform silver color palette relies on contrasting textures—dense glitter, sheer mesh, and reflective crystals—to create depth and brilliant visual prominence.
