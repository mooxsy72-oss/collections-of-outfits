OUTFIT: outfit features an animal-themed fantasy aesthetic, defined by its contrasting components of form-fitting black base garments and oversized, voluminous faux-fur outerwear accessories. The primary visual pattern is a dense, high-contrast leopard print in shades of tan, brown, and black, which dominates the outermost layers. A solid matte black covers the core bodysuit.The base garment consists of a tightly fitted, strapless, one-piece black romper with a sweetheart neckline and short, form-fitting boy-short bottoms. This stretchy knit piece leaves the entire upper chest, shoulders, collarbones, and thighs fully exposed.Oversized, exaggerated accessories define the silhouette. A dramatic, heavy faux-fur hood lines the head, extending downward via long, thin black cords ending in plush triangular tips. The hood features pointed, upright animal ears lined with structured black inner material. Separate, bulky fingerless gauntlets made of the same thick leopard-print faux fur wrap the lower forearms.A wide, rigid black leatherette belt sits at the hips, detailed with a silver rectangular buckle and two parallel rows of metallic silver grommets. Extending from the hips are flared, floor-length chaps-style leg warmers. These pieces feature a dramatic, ultra-wide bell-bottom flare that drapes heavily over the feet, accented by curved black plush claws at the front base. A long, stuffed plush tail attached to the back of the belt sweeps down to the floor.
