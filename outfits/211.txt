OUTFIT: fantasy fairycore ensemble features a structured, strapless mini dress shaped like an upside-down blossom, creating a highly sculptural, top-heavy silhouette with dramatic, tiered lower volume. The overall color palette is a soft, monochromatic pastel pink, accented by shimmering details and white embellishments.The garment consists of a rigid, heavily structured bodice that is skin-tight and fits snugly from above the bust down to the natural waist. It features a strapless sweetheart neckline with a shallow center dip and wide chest coverage, leaving the shoulders, collarbones, upper back, and arms fully exposed.At the natural waist, the dress dramatically flares out into a voluminous, structured balloon-style mini skirt that ends at the upper thigh. The skirt is constructed from multiple overlapping, petal-shaped panels arranged in tiers, mimicking the organic layering of a flower. The wide pattern cuts, stiff materials, and internal hoop or crinoline structure give the skirt maximum fullness and rigid shape retention.The fabric is a lightweight, translucent organza or tulle with a subtle metallic sheen, layered over an opaque pink base. The bodice and petal edges are densely embellished with purple and iridescent sequins, 3D floral appliqués, and crystal beading. Suspended from the bodice sides and trailing from the lower petal hems are multiple long, delicate bead-strand tendrils that extend down past the knees to the mid-calf. These strands are strung with tiny white faux pearls and small, white 3D butterfly and blossom accents. A single delicate silver chain necklace with a small drop pendant rests at the base of the neck.
