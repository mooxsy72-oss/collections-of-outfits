OUTFIT: a fantasy costume blends elements of a theatrical 18th-century Baroque court dress with animalistic fox folklore. The silhouette features an exaggerated, bell-shaped mini skirt and a heavily structured, corseted torso. The dominant colors are muted terracotta orange and antiqued gold, accented by dark bronze and cream.The main garment consists of a tightly fitted, strapless corset bodice with a scalloped bottom edge, crafted from a stiff, heavyweight gold-brocade fabric. It features intricate, dense vertical embroidery down the center front using bronze beads. Attached to the top are short, voluminous puff sleeves made of sheer, lightweight brown-dotted tulle, which end mid-bicep with ruffled cuffs, leaving the shoulders, collarbones, and upper chest fully exposed. Below the waist sits an ultra-short, highly voluminous balloon skirt made of finely gathered, lightweight terracotta cotton. It is supported by an underlying structured petticoat to hold its rigid, rounded shape, and its hem is trimmed with a dense row of small, spherical pom-poms.Accessories include a multi-layered cream chiffon ruffle collar fitted tightly around the neck. The footwear consists of pointed-toe pumps wrapped in bronze glitter, featuring a thick trim of fluffy, light brown fur around the ankles. A long, oversized, highly voluminous faux-fur fox tail in vibrant orange and white attaches at the back, draping down to the floor as a major visual focal point.
