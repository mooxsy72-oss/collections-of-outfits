OUTFIT: outfit belongs to the contemporary utilitarian techwear fashion style, characterized by functional tactical gear, multi-pocket cargo garments, and a monochromatic palette. Matte black is the dominant color across the upper torso, head, and chest rig, while a desaturated dark teal-blue serves as the secondary color on the trousers. The entire look is devoid of graphic patterns, prioritizing solid textures.The upper garment is a tight-fitting, short-sleeved crewneck t-shirt made of lightweight, stretchy cotton jersey that closely contours the torso, exposing the forearms. The lower garment consists of mid-weight, structured cotton-twill cargo pants featuring a relaxed, straight-leg silhouette that stacks slightly above the ankles. The pants feature a mid-rise waist with belt loops, integrated into a thick black nylon webbed tactical belt with a heavy-duty metallic quick-release buckle. Large, bulky three-dimensional cargo pockets are symmetrically placed on the outer thighs, with a small rectangular compass-style brand patch affixed to the left pocket flap.The silhouette is visually dominated by a prominent matte black nylon chest rig pack, which features adjustable vertical and horizontal webbing straps crossing the chest, anchored by plastic ladder-lock buckles. The central rectangular pouch rests on the mid-torso, constructed with zippered compartments and a zipper pull tab. Headwear consists of a classic structured black cotton twill six-panel baseball cap with a curved brim, detailed with a small embroidered silver logo on the front left panel. A seamless matte black fabric face mask covers the lower face, stretching from the bridge of the nose to under the chin. A metallic gold link watch with a round face is worn on the left wrist.
