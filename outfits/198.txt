OUTFIT: fantasy high-medieval knight armor style features a dominant polished silver steel color palette, highlighted by secondary gold-trimmed borders and a dark maroon-red back cape. The rigid, metallic silhouette completely covers the body except for the inner elbows, groin, and upper thighs, where a form-fitting black fabric arming doublet and chausses are exposed.The structure is highly voluminous due to layered, rigid metal components. A multi-plated, articulated silver breastplate covers the torso, secured with adjustable brown leather shoulder straps. It connects to a flared fauld at the waist. The shoulders are exaggerated by oversized, rounded pauldrons featuring intricate, engraved gold filigree borders along the top ridges. Segmented vambraces and fully articulated steel gauntlets enclose the arms. The lower body layers heavy, pointed poleyns over the knees and matching silver greaves over the shins, all finished with distinct gold-painted trim lines. Footwear consists of pointed, articulated steel sabatons with slightly upturned toes. A long, unpatterned maroon-red cape attaches to the back collar, draping loosely to the floor with heavy, fluid folds. The head is entirely hidden by a rounded close helm featuring a fluted slitted visor.
