OUTFIT: This ensemble aligns with the futuristic techwear and avant-garde subcultural fashion movements, characterized by modularity, utilitarian hardware, and subverted garment construction. The monochrome palette consists of dominant off-white and pale gray, with secondary accents of light tan and silver metal.The upper body features a lightweight, cropped white base top with a finely ribbed texture, leaving the entire midriff exposed. Layered over it is an extremely cropped, ultra-high-neck jacket structure that functions primarily as a modular collar and sleeve unit. This structured garment boasts an oversized, stiffly padded funnel neck that covers the chin, transitioning into wide, voluminous sleeves that bunch at the forearms.The lower body is defined by highly exaggerated, multi-part trousers. The upper portion forms a high-waisted, V-shaped brief construction with an exposed front zipper and triangular cutouts at the hips, completely exposing the upper thighs. This section connects via internal snaps or straps to oversized, baggy pant legs made of heavily textured, coarse gray mesh fabric. These detached legs bulge outwards to create immense visual volume before tucking into bulky, heavily padded off-white snowboarding boots featuring prominent dial-lacing systems. The silhouette's absolute focal point is an oversized, asymmetrical tactical headset accessory. It features a matte white padded headband with subtle white technical text branding near the temple. The right ear cup is replaced by an extremely complex, mechanical wing-like structure built from rigid, molded plastic or polymer plates. This attachment consists of multiple elongated, spiked fins and angular panels in a contrasting white and gray color scheme, giving the illusion of integrated mecha cyberware.
