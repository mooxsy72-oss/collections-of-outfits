OUTFIT: A two-piece fantasy fairy ensemble features a dominant bright aqua-blue color palette with a sparkling, textured finish, accented by a lighter semi-translucent mint green in the wings. The most visually dominant elements are the glittering fabrics, the severe angular cutouts, and the heavily stylized, exaggerated proportions of the footwear and wings.The top is a sleeveless, fitted halter crop top with an asymmetric, diagonally sliced hemline that exposes the midriff. It features a high, form-fitting neckline. The matching low-rise mini skirt has a straight silhouette, a short side slit on the right thigh. Both garments are crafted from a highly structured, shape-retaining medium-weight fabric densely covered in a fine silver glitter pattern resembling a scattered starfield.Matching aqua-blue fingerless glove-sleeves extend from the mid-forearm to pointed tips over the backs of the hands, fitting tightly to the skin. On the feet are chunky, oversized platform ankle boots featuring a glossy, smooth leather-like texture, exaggerated square toes, and thick block heels. A small, simple gold tiara with three rounded peaks sits atop the head as a rigid, metallic accessory. Sprouting from the back are large, delicate, semi-translucent mint green wings framed in thin white borders, consisting of two larger upper segments and two smaller rounded lower segments that create a wide, dramatic silhouette.
