OUTFIT: fantasy rococo-gothic ensemble uses black as the dominant color, ivory-white as the secondary color, and dense, metallic gold filigree patterns. The silhouette is extremely elongated, dominated by a voluminous floor-length cape, balloon sleeves, and skin-tight trousers.The inner layer is an ivory-white silk poet blouse with a high, structured wing collar, a densely tucked pleated bib, and oversized balloon sleeves gathered at the forearms. Over this sits a black velvet corset vest with a scalloped, pointed underbust waistline and integrated suspender-like straps, heavily embroidered with gold floral filigree. The trousers are ultra-slim black matte stretch fabric, extending into integrated pointed-toe boots that completely cover the lower body. Dense gold rococo embroidery wraps around the hips, outer thighs, and shins, accented by hanging gold chain swags, small cross pendants, and tassels.Draped from the structured, padded shoulders is a massive black wool cape with a stiffened wing collar. The cape's interior lining features a large-scale, ornate gold and grey damask pattern that flows down to its trailing, asymmetrical hem. Wearable accessories include clawed black leather gloves wrapped in gold chains and a slim gold-topped dress cane.
