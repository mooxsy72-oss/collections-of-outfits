This classification is supported by the combination of a structured corset, voluminous pleated ruffles, delicate lace, and whimsical, dark-themed wearable appendages like wings and a tail. The color composition is predominantly deep black and midnight purple, punctuated by a white collar and small gold accents. Visual attention is primarily drawn to the highly structured corset bodice, the sheer volume of the micro-skirt ruffles, and the intricate tattered detailing on the thigh-high hosiery.The primary full-body garment is a highly structured, skintight romper ensemble centered around a strapless corset bodice. The bodice features alternating vertical panels of deep purple and matte black fabric, cinching tightly at the waist with a pointed lower hem. Wearable fantasy accessories attached to the upper back include a pair of small, structured leathery bat wings with a dark purple gradient lining, accented by small silver hoops pierced through the wing tips, and a long, flexible black demonic tail ending in a stylized spade tip.
