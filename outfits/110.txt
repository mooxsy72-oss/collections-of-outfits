OUTFIT: outfit is a high-contrast villainous ensemble inspired by the fictional character Cruella de Vil, characterized by a dominant black-and-white color palette with striking pops of crimson red and silver accents.The centerpiece is an oversized, floor-length faux-fur duster coat featuring a dalmatian-print pattern of black spots on a stark white background. This heavyweight coat has exaggerated, bulky shoulders, ultra-wide lapels, and voluminous long sleeves that create massive visual weight. Beneath it sits a tailored black leather jacket with structured lapels, layered over a fluid, lightweight black silk button-down shirt. The shirt is worn open deep at the neckline, revealing a bare chest decorated with a thin silver chain necklace. Matching black leather straight-leg trousers continue the monochrome look down the legs, held up by a smooth black leather belt with a square silver buckle and an attached silver trouser wallet chain draped across the hip.Accents are strictly coordinated to enhance the dramatic theme. The hands are covered in form-fitting, smooth crimson red leather gloves that provide a sharp contrast to the monochrome garments. On top of the gloves, chunky silver metal statement rings are worn on the fingers. The look is completed with a structured, matte black masquerade eye mask that wraps around the upper face, paired with a slim, rigid black costume cane held in the right hand. All items fit smoothly to the body, contrasting with the immense volume of the outer fur coat.
