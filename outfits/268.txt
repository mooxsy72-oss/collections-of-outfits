Underneath sits a thick, structured cream-colored crewneck sweatshirt with a ribbed collar and waistband, ending just below the beltline. They feature horizontal knee seams and a wide, relaxed leg silhouette.An oversized, extra-long scarf is wrapped around the neck. It is made of a thick, fuzzy, brushed mohair-style knit featuring a large-scale plaid pattern in shades of sage, forest green, and cream, finished with thick, twisted fringe. The outfit is completed by a minimalist black cotton baseball cap and black athletic sneakers with contrasting white stripes and white socks.
