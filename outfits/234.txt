OUTFIT: warrior ensemble is rooted in stylized Norse and Germanic mythological clothing traditions, characterized by a completely bare upper body, intricate runic tattoos, and wrapped leg garments. The palette utilizes dark charcoal, muted tan, and reddish-brown leathers, with no fabric surface patterns.Extensive dark gray body art covers the entire torso, back, shoulders, and arms. The chest features a large concentric circular design on the right pectoral and a smaller circular motif on the left. Ribbon-like bands containing linear runes wrap diagonally across the ribs, down both bicep areas, and fully coat the right forearm.The lower half features a high-waisted, gathered gray fabric sash wrapped tightly around the midriff. Over it sits an asymmetric, single-panel tattered loincloth of distressed tan suede with a jagged, pointed hemline. This is secured by a slim, reddish-brown leather belt featuring a carved silver metallic strap end dangling down the front.The trousers are dark gray, baggy braccae that balloon through the thighs before tapering at the knees. The lower legs are tightly bound in crisscrossing reddish-brown leather leg wraps, compressing the fabric underneath. Footwear consists of simple, open-toed leather turnshoes or sandals wrapped around the feet.
