OUTFIT: This attire belongs to the outdated Northern Russian folk clothing tradition, specifically reflecting a woman's winter ensemble from the Kargopol region. This classification is supported by the heavy, structured outerwear, a full-length under-skirt, a traditional headscarf arrangement, and the inclusion of distinctive regional elements like a front-running fur band. The ensemble relies on a high-contrast color scheme dominated by deep matte navy blue, with a secondary vibrant primary red. A multi-colored, abstractly patterned textile anchors the head covering, while natural brown serves as a textural accent. The fabrics are strictly solid-colored without geometric or floral surface prints. Shuba (Outer Coat): A heavyweight, densely structured winter coat made of dark navy wool with a matte, slightly brushed texture. It features a fitted body that terminates at a natural, reinforced waist seam. From this seam, a full, heavily pleated A-line skirt extends down to the lower calves. The neckline is high, rounded, and finished with a narrow trim. Long, slim, straight-cut sleeves end in turned-back cuffs trimmed with short-pile, light brown fur. The vertical front closure wraps slightly and is completely concealed beneath a wide, continuous band of natural long-pile brown fur running from the left collar bone straight down to the front hemline.Sarafan or Podorozhnik (Under-Skirt): Visible below the hem of the coat is a floor-length, voluminous skirt element in deep crimson red. It has a stiff, structured drape with prominent vertical folds and features a solid, smooth texture. Povoynik and Platok (Headwear Ensemble): A multi-layered head covering consisting of a fitted, solid crimson-red inner cap or under-scarf wrapped tightly over the brow. Layered directly over this is a thick, woolen, voluminous headscarf with an intricate, multi-colored, dense abstract pattern mixing dark teal, olive green, and earthy tones. The scarf wraps under the chin and bundles thickly around the base of the neck, forming a heavy, protective collar-like ring.
