OUTFIT: ensemble features deep midnight blue as the dominant color, accented by secondary tones of silver, charcoal, and ice-blue. It belongs to a high-fantasy winter royalty clothing tradition, indicated by the exaggerated, highly structured silhouettes, opulently dense silver beadwork, and plush fur trims. Visual attention focuses on the densely embellished, corseted bodice and the massive volume of the pooling skirt.

Full-body garment: A floor-length ballgown is crafted from heavy, plush midnight blue velvet that retains structural fullness and shape. The upper bodice is closely fitted and patterned like a structured corset, featuring a high, rigid mandarin collar. The center bodice is densely covered in silver and pewter-toned floral filigree embroidery, rhinestones, and metallic beadwork that tapers down toward a sharply pointed basque waist. The expansive, floor-sweeping bell-shaped skirt is supported by structured underskirts, with symmetrical cascading panels of silver beadwork and baroque vine embroidery trailing down the front.

Outerwear: A dramatic, floor-length matching midnight blue velvet open-front cape rests on the shoulders, which are heavily built up with large, voluminous tufts of dark charcoal and silver fox faux fur. The inner panels and trailing edges of the heavy cape are covered in dense, intricate tone-on-tone blue and silver botanical embroidery.
