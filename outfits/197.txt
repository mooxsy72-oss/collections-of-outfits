OUTFIT: outfit represents a full-coverage, street-casual ensemble heavily associated with post-Soviet "gopnik" subculture, identified by its specific pairing of a matching athletic tracksuit and a low-profile beanie. The color palette is strictly split between a dominant light cream upper body and secondary deep black for the legs and head, accented by crisp white and dark navy details.The upper layer consists of a zip-up athletic jacket with a relaxed, slightly slouchy silhouette and ribbed, elasticated funnel collar, cuffs, and hem. It is made from a lightweight, smooth synthetic polyester knit that drapes softly but retains its shape at the structured seams. Three distinct parallel dark stripes run continuously down the length of the sleeves.The lower layer consists of matching straight-leg athletic trousers featuring an elastic waistband. Made from a similar smooth synthetic fabric, they hang loosely over the shoes, with three white parallel stripes running down the outer sides of each leg.Footwear consists of low-top retro sneakers with white leather uppers, grey suede toe overlays, and dark navy stripes on the sides. A tightly knit, fold-over black beanie sits closely against the head as the single accessory.
