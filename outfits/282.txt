OUTFIT: gothic fantasy outfit combines Victorian and steampunk elements into an asymmetric, high-low ballgown silhouette. The dominant color is deep burgundy, supported by stark black and metallic silver accents.The bodice is a structured, strapless, sweetheart-neckline corset with a pointed waistline. It is crafted from stiff, medium-weight burgundy brocade with vertical boning channels, featuring a central line of silver filigree buttons and crystal embellishments. This leaves the shoulders, collarbones, upper chest, and back fully exposed.The voluminous skirt features a high-low construction, short and layered at the front to expose the thighs and legs, while draping into a long, heavy floor-length train at the back. It layers a black lace-trimmed underskirt beneath gathered swags of thick, lustrous burgundy velvet and black satin, creating deep, dramatic folds and immense visual weight.Accessories include elbow-length sheer black lace gloves covering the arms, sheer black thigh-high stockings with lace cuffs, and silver glitter-encrusted platform heels with pearl-embellished ankle straps. A ornate silver choker with a central teardrop ruby stone serves as a primary focal point at the neck.
