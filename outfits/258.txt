OUTFIT: minimalist tactical outfit leans heavily into a stylized military aesthetic, leaving the entire torso completely bare. The color distribution is purely monochromatic military camouflage, dominated by tan, brown, and olive drab tones.The sole garment is a pair of rugged, loose-fitting combat trousers. They feature a straight-leg silhouette with medium volume and slight stacking at the ankles. The trousers are constructed from a heavy, stiff ripstop cotton-blend fabric that retains its shape, bunching rigidly at the knees and lower leg. The entire garment features a dense, multi-terrain camouflage pattern. Cargo pockets with flap closures add structured volume to the outer thighs.The bare upper body emphasizes the bulky, visually dominant accessories. A wide, stiff tactical battle belt in coyote tan circles the waist, heavily loaded with modular webbed pouches. On the head sits a structured baseball cap covered in matching camouflage fabric, integrated with large, thick olive drab over-ear hearing protection muffs. The hands are covered by form-fitting, tan tactical shooting gloves made of thin, flexible synthetic suede with reinforced fabric across the knuckles.
