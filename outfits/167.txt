OUTFIT: outfit belongs to a fantasy fairy clothing tradition inspired by the character Flora from Winx Club, characterized by organic, plant-like shapes and brilliant textures. The dominant color is a shimmering magenta-pink, with a secondary palette of pastel lime-green, iridescent silver, and lavender.The hairstyle features exceptionally long, flowing hair reaching past the thighs, styled into a braided crown that wraps around the head. Two thick, structured braids drape over the shoulders, while the remaining hair cascades down the back and sides in loose, voluminous waves. Curving curtain bangs frame the face. The main garment is a highly structured, skin-tight asymmetrical mini-dress with a plunge neckline and transparent mesh panels across the midriff. The dress is constructed from a heavy, densely glittered magenta fabric. It features prominent, three-dimensional overlapping leaf appliqués that form the bust cups and flare outward along the hips, creating a stylized petal skirt. These leaf shapes are outlined with deep magenta borders. Skin-tight gauntlets cover the forearms, matching the dress with identical glittering fabric and upward-pointing leaf shapes at the elbows. A silver collar necklace, adorned with leaf-shaped links and a central teardrop crystal pendant, sits tightly against the throat. On the lower legs, matching knee-high boots repeat the overlapping leaf motif along their upper rims.Oversized, delicate wings dominate the silhouette, extending wider than the torso and taller than the head. Composed of four pointed, translucent segments, they display an iridescent color gradient shifting from pastel pink at the edges to soft green near the center. Inner gold veins mimic leaf patterns, and small pink flower charms dangle from the lower wingtips on delicate silver chains. A dense aura of floating, luminous silver particles and twinkling fairy dust surrounds the entire ensemble. The overall design elements convey a mood of ethereal enchantment and woodland mysticism.
