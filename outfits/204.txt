OUTFIT: high-fashion ensemble subverts traditional mid-20th-century menswear tailoring through intentionally exaggerated, oversized proportions and dramatic layering, providing full body coverage.The dominant color is a muted, desaturated tan-brown, contrasted by a secondary deep chocolate brown and accented with stark cream and dark maroon. An oversized, waist-length flight jacket in distressed, heavy-weight chocolate brown leather dominates the upper silhouette. It features dropped shoulders, voluminous sleeves with rolled-back cream-lined cuffs, broad notched lapels, and prominent utility chest pockets. Underneath sits a slouchy, double-breasted suit jacket in mid-weight, softly draping tan wool with low-set buttons and relaxed lapels.The suit jacket layers over a crisp, stiff-collared white dress shirt and a narrow, smooth silk tie in dark maroon with a diagonal satin stripe. The lower body is defined by extremely voluminous, wide-leg trousers matching the tan wool suit fabric. They feature deep front pleats that create an ultra-baggy silhouette, pooling heavily over the footwear. Accessories include dark brown leather gloves tucked into the sleeves, and substantial, thick-soled black leather loafers with a rounded, structured toe box.
