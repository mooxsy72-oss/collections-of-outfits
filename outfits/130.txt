OUTFIT: fantasy-historical warrior costume blends early medieval Viking and Anglo-Saxon aesthetics, utilizing heavy textures, structured layering, and geometric panels to create a bulky, imposing silhouette. The dominant color palette is dark brown and deep olive green, accented by an orange-red waist sash and weathered gold or bronze metal elements. The massive, shaggy fur mantle and the highly detailed, paneled leather tunic draw the most visual attention.
The outermost layer features a voluminous, long-haired black fur mantle that drapes heavily across the shoulders, widening the upper torso. Beneath this rests a structured, knee-length leather split-skirt tunic in dark brown. The center front panel of the tunic is decorated with stacked, downward-pointing chevrons stitched onto olive-green fabric, flanked by vertical rows of small, square metallic rivets. The collar forms a high, overlapping neckband. Long, dark green sleeves extend down the arms, capped at the forearms by wide leather bracers featuring a debossed grid pattern and soft fur-lined trim. At the waist, a wide, densely woven orange-and-black patterned textile sash provides a sharp pop of color. It is securely overlaid with a thick, dark brown leather belt that features a prominent brass buckle, a long hanging strap with a metallic tip, and an attached leather pouch on the right hip. Below the tunic's segmented hem, a dark green underskirt flows down, meeting structured, knee-high black leather boots that are heavily weathered with mud. A bronze wheel-shaped pendant hangs flat against the upper chest.
