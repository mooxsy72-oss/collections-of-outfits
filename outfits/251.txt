OUTFIT: fantasy-armor gown draws from medieval European plate and chainmail traditions, featuring a rigid metallic bodice, an armored gauntlet, and a metallic mesh skirt.The silhouette is dominated by an elongated, highly structured strapless silver metal corset. It features sculpted anatomical breast cups and an elongated, pointed waist that curves over the hips. The metal is smooth, reflective, and completely rigid with a mirror-like finish. A dark, ornate, three-dimensional crucifix motif is vertically centered on the abdomen. The corset leaves the shoulders, arms, collarbones, and upper back entirely bare.The secondary element is a lightweight, fluid chainmail maxi skirt made from dense, metallic silver rings. The material behaves like a heavy fluid, draping close to the hips before falling to the floor into a trailing pool of fabric. A dramatic thigh-high slit on the wearer's right completely exposes the leg.An oversized, articulated silver metal gauntlet covers the right forearm and hand. It features a rigid, flared bracer at the forearm and segmented plates over the hand, contrasting sharply with the bare left arm.
