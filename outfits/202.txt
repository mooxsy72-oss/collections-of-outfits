OUTFIT: beast-themed costume features five distinct components: a cropped vest, two oversized paw gloves, a headband, and trousers. The primary color palette consists of multi-toned brown, tan, and black faux fur, contrasted against solid matte black trousers.The cropped vest is open-front, sleeveless, and hits mid-chest, leaving the entire torso, midriff, and arms exposed. It is constructed from thick, high-pile faux fur with a variegated tan and dark brown striped pattern that mimics wild animal pelts. This heavy fabric possesses significant structure and volume, holding its shape away from the body without draping.The matching oversized paw gloves extend to the mid-forearm, creating an intentionally bulky, disproportionate silhouette. The back of the hands features the same striped faux fur. The palm of the left glove reveals a smooth, pink fabric patch shaped like a stylized animal paw pad, tipped with sharp, rigid black plastic claws.A black plush tail with a wire core curves upward from the waist, while a simple black headband holds two triangular, upward-pointing feline ears made of black faux fur with pink inner linings. The look is grounded by loose-fitting, high-waisted black trousers in a lightweight woven fabric.
