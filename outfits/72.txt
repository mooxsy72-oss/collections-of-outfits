OUTFIT: a fantasy-adventure ensemble combines Renaissance pirate and modern gothic elements, defined by an extreme contrast in texture, volume, and material behavior.The upper half features a lightweight, off-the-shoulder chemise blouse in a textured, cream-colored linen or cotton weave. It features a deeply gathered, wide elasticated neckline with a small central keyhole drawstring tie, exposing the collarbones and shoulders. The sleeves are heavily voluminous, billowy poet-style sleeves that gather at the forearm into long, tight, smocked cuffs extending to the wrists.The top is tucked tightly into a high-waisted, ultra-fitted corset or pant hybrid made from heavy-weight, high-shine black latex or PVC material that reflects sharp light highlights. This structured, non-drape material tightly hugs the hips and thighs down the legs. It features decorative lacing details along the outer thighs and a prominent raised seam construction at the crotch.Footwear consists of knee-high, structured cream leather boots with a flat sole. They feature a full front lace-up closure with dark brown leather laces, overlapping straps, and silver metal buckles near the top calf. A wide, dark brown leather utility belt with attached pouches wraps the waist, securing a prop sword hilt. A wide, thick dark leather cuff wraps the right wrist.
