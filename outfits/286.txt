OUTFIT: ensemble blends a dark romantic fantasy aesthetic with elements inspired by the 18th-century Rococo period and the late Victorian era, as evidenced by its tightly cinched corsetry, exaggerated pannier-like peplum hip ruffles, and dramatic leg-of-mutton puff sleeves. The palette is dominated by rich, deep copper-bronze and dark brown ornate damask brocade, contrasted against deep forest green silk and semi-translucent dark slate blue.The upper body features a structured, low-cut corset bodice with a sweetheart neckline, constructed from a heavy, rigid copper and dark brown damask fabric. The shoulders and short sleeves are highly exaggerated, forming voluminous, structured puffs that gather above the elbow with ruffled cuffs. A matching brocade peplum flares sharply from the waist, mimicking historical hip panniers. At the neck, a matching brocade choker collar features a tall, ruffled edge and long fabric ties draping over the bare collarbone area.The lower body consists of high-waisted, relaxed-fit forest green satin shorts with a smooth, reflective texture, finished along the straight hem with fine black lace trim. A wide, smooth dark brown leather belt firmly cinches the natural waist. The legs are covered in smooth, semi-translucent slate blue tights, leading down to platform ankle boots upholstered in the same copper and dark brown brocade fabric, tied with a matching patterned bow at the front ankle. Accessories include elbow-length, form-fitting dark brown leather gloves with voluminous ruffled cuffs at the wrists, and a medium-sized, unstructured tote bag made of matching mottled copper-bronze brocade with a dark metallic chain shoulder strap.
