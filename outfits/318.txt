OUTFIT: monochrome ensemble relies completely on solid matte and semi-gloss black textures, creating a uniform, dark color palette across all layers. Visual attention is driven by the stark contrast between the heavy, oversized proportions of the upper outerwear and the form-fitting silhouette of the lower legs.An underlying matte black knit dress serves as the base layer, featuring a high, tight turtleneck collar and a very short hemline that cuts straight across the upper thighs. It is cinched at the natural waist by a narrow black leather belt with a small, metallic silver buckle. Over this sits an exaggerated, oversized black leather jacket with dropped shoulders and loose, voluminous sleeves that taper into gathered cuffs. The jacket features a high-reaching funnel collar secured with a throat latch, a wide asymmetrical front flap, and an elasticized ribbed leather hemline that rests low on the hips.The legs are covered by sheer, lightweight black hosiery that reveals a hint of skin tone beneath. Over this, wide-shaft, over-the-knee boots in soft black leather rise to the mid-thigh. They feature a slouchy, unstructured silhouette that wrinkles heavily around the ankles, a squared-off toe box, and low, chunky block heels.Accessories include a structured black baseball cap adorned with white embroidered text across the front panel. Smooth black leather gloves cover the hands, paired with a chunky, sculptural silver metallic cuff bracelet worn over the wrist of the glove. Small, rounded silver button earrings accent the ears, and dark, opaque black sunglasses obscure the eyes. Under the arm sits a structured, matte black leather handbag with a top handle and a prominent silver metallic turn-lock clasp on the front flap.
