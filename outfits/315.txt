OUTFIT: outfit balances a structured, equestrian-inspired silhouette with soft, romantic details, utilizing a rich espresso-brown and cream color palette. The visual composition features a highly structured jacket that shapes the upper torso, contrasted against fluid, light-colored layers underneath and prominent knee-high boots that draw immediate focus to the lower half.The base layer consists of a relaxed-fit, semi-translucent cream blouse or shirt-dress crafted from a lightweight, fluid woven fabric. It features a prominent pointed collar worn open at the neck, and long sleeves with relaxed cuffs that extend past the outerwear. The straight hemline finishes at the upper thigh and is heavily embellished with an delicate, semi-sheer white floral lace trim. Layered over this is a tailored, structural jacket in a solid matte espresso-brown woven suiting fabric with high shape retention. It is built with straight, lightly padded shoulders, regular long sleeves, and a deep V-neckline that frames the inner collar. The silhouette features a cinched waistline defined by stitched radiating pleats that flare dramatically into a peplum-style split hem, closing down the center with four large, matching fabric-covered round buttons. Sheer, dark brown hosiery covers the legs.Footwear consists of slouchy, knee-high boots in a smooth, matte cream-colored leather or faux leather. They feature a structured pointed toe, a high shaft that sits loose against the leg, and a hidden or low heel. Accessories are kept minimal, including a small, structured asymmetrical shoulder bag in dark espresso-brown leather with a short strap and minimal gold hardware, alongside a delicate, thin gold chain necklace.
