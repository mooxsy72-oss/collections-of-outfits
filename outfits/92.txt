OUTFIT: ensemble is a stylized military dress uniform drawing inspiration from 19th-century European regimental attire, indicated by the double-breasted tunic, stand-up collar, epaulets, and hussar-style frogging. The color palette is strictly defined by dominant white, secondary deep black, and bright metallic gold accents.The primary garment is a structured, hip-length tunic jacket made from a thick, smooth white wool blend that retains a rigid shape. It features a high, stiff mandarin collar trimmed in black fabric and gold piping, straight-cut long sleeves ending in thick black cuffs with tiered gold braid trim, and structured padded shoulders topped with gold braided epaulets. A decorative wide black fabric sash wraps diagonally from the left shoulder to the right hip, embellished with a large gold embroidered laurel wreath motif near the shoulder. Double-breasted gold bar frogging with spherical metallic buttons details the chest.Matching white, slim-fit trousers provide full body coverage, tapering into knee-high, structured black leather riding boots with a slight heel. Draped over the right shoulder is a heavy, voluminous black velvet cape that extends down to the mid-calf. This cape features a thick, bulky collar made of dense black faux fur and is bordered with a wide, intricate gold braided trim along its open front edges.
