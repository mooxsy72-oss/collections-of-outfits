OUTFIT: This evening gown blends modern formalwear with romantic fantasy elements. The palette features a dominant deep burgundy-deep purple base layer paired with a secondary sheer black overlay. The silhouette is heavily dominated by an exaggerated, form-fitting mermaid shape that contrasts a tightly cinched torso with a high-volume, floor-sweeping lower skirt.The main garment is an off-the-shoulder mermaid gown. The bodice fits skin-tightly down the torso, hips, and upper thighs. It exposes the entire upper chest, collarbones, and shoulders, while completely covering the midriff, hips, and legs. Around the waist, thin bands of sheer black fabric wrap horizontally to visually compress the midriff. At the lower thighs, the skirt flares effectively, utilizing gathered fabric and heavy layering to create an expansive, pooling hemline that pools on the floor.The gown is constructed from two distinct materials. The inner layer is a thick, structured, opaque fabric in a rich burgundy shade that provides shape retention. Over this sits a lightweight, sheer black chiffon or tulle overlay that softens the color beneath, creating a smoky, bi-color appearance. The sleeves are a secondary focal point, constructed entirely from the sheer black fabric. They fit tightly along the forearms before flaring into dramatic, oversized bell cuffs that add significant volume and ripple over the hands. A delicate, thin metallic chain necklace with a small, indistinct pendant sits subtly against the exposed collarbone as the sole accessory.
