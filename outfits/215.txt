OUTFIT: fantasy Valentine or Cupid costume relies on dramatic textures and iconic holiday motifs, dominated by deep crimson red and accented by bright white.The main garment is a form-fitting, ultra-short velvet romper with a paneled bodice that exposes the arms, collarbones, upper chest, and thighs. It features a sweetheart neckline lined with white lace trim, split down the center front by a vertical white lace insertion dotted with small amber beads. Elasticized, highly gathered white organza ruffles form delicate, voluminous off-the-shoulder straps. The waist is tightly defined by a long, wide white satin ribbon tied into a prominent bow with trailing ends. The leg openings are dramatically flared and voluminous, constructed from gathered red velvet that is heavily lined and extended by multiple stiff layers of dense, ruffled white organza trim.A pair of large, highly structured crimson red feathered wings extends outward from the back, acting as the silhouette's primary focal point. A thin red headband supports two upright, rigid red velvet heart shapes outlined in sparkling white rhinestones. The hand accessories include a long white arrow with a large, fluffy white feather fletching and a carved crescent-shaped bone tip.
