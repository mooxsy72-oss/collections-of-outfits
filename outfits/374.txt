OUTFIT: ensemble channels a high-fantasy merchant prince or regal Byzantine-inspired aristocratic aesthetic, defined by heavy layering, opulent textiles, and flowing, grand proportions. The dominant colors are deep shades of black, dark plum or purple, and antique gold, accented by an off-white underlayer. Visual attention is anchored by the immense volume of the flowing outerwear robe and the dense, shimmering gold embroidery concentrated along the garment edges and waistline.The base upper-body layer is a lightweight, fluid off-white silk or fine linen shirt, worn loose and heavily unbuttoned down the center chest. It features a soft, unstructured standing collar and long, relaxed sleeves that subtly bunch around the wrists. Wrapped firmly over the shirt and trouser waistline is a wide, cummerbund-style waist sash crafted from a dark purple and gold metallic brocade fabric, featuring an intricate, dense jacquard weave.The lower-body garments consist of slim-fit, tailored black trousers made from a smooth, mid-weight woven material that tucks directly into the boots.The primary layer is an oversized, floor-length open-front robe or kaftan made from heavy, plush black or deep plum velvet. This dramatic garment features immensely wide, floor-sweeping sleeves that create draped, wing-like volume. The entire front opening, hemline, and wide sleeve cuffs are heavily bordered with wide bands of intricate antique gold embroidery in a dense botanical or filigree pattern. Hanging gold tassels accentuate the sleeve corners and front edges.Footwear comprises tall knee-high riding boots made of polished, semi-glossy black leather with a rounded toe, accented with thick gold embroidered borders along the top rims. A delicate gold chain necklace with a small pendant rests on the exposed chest.
