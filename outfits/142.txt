OUTFIT: ensemble blends Gothic fashion with Japanese Dark Lolita aesthetics, using extreme proportions and heavy ornamentation. The dominant color is stark black, contrasted by a secondary creamy white/ivory tutu and white faux pearls.The upper half features a rigid, tight-fitting black velvet corset-bodice with a straight neckline, exposing the chest and collarbones. Caricature-like, oversized pagoda sleeves explosion out at the shoulders in dense, multi-layered black ruffles, tapering into sheer black chiffon long sleeves. The focal point of the lower silhouette is a hyper-voluminous, ultra-short ivory tulle tutu skirt. It sits tightly at the waist and uses densely gathered, stiff mesh to flare straight outward, exposing the entire thigh and leg. Underneath, a delicate layer of scalloped cream lace shorts provides minimal coverage.Massive accessories heavily dominate the chest and legs. A thick black fabric neck-choker connects to multiple cascading, multi-layered strands of large faux pearl necklaces draping down the torso. On the lower half, black over-the-knee socks are covered in a dense, random pattern of small white pearls and wrapped at the shins with bulky, matching pearl strands.
