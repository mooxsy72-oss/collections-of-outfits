OUTFIT: high-fantasy, runway-style costume relies entirely on texture and exaggerated volume, showcasing a monochromatic bright white palette contrasted by bare skin.The silhouette is dominated by an immense pair of floor-length, oversized angel wings that tower over the head and trail down the steps. The wings are heavily layered with long, wispy, lightweight ostrich or faux feathers that catch the air, creating massive visual volume and structure while remaining soft.The central garment is a strapless, ultra-short corset mini dress. The bodice features structured, semi-sheer vertical boning panels with defined underwire cups, meeting at a low-dipping sweetheart neckline. The waist is tightly cinched, bursting into a highly voluminous, tutu-like micro skirt made entirely of dense, fluffy marabou or faux feathers that flare outward. This design leaves the shoulders, collarbones, arms, back, and the full length of the legs entirely exposed.Footwear consists of minimalist high-heeled stiletto sandals. They feature a thin, transparent PVC front strap across the toes and a nude or clear high stiletto heel, creating an illusion of elongated, bare legs that balances the heavy volume of the feathers above.
