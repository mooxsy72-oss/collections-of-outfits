OUTFIT: outfit is a highly structured, avant-garde full-body bodysuit ensemble with a dominant stark white and ivory color scheme, completely embellished with layered pearl details that create a dense, regal, and sculptural visual texture. The design blends elements of high-fashion evening wear with traditional South Asian bridal aesthetics, as evidenced by the specific placement of the forehead and neck ornaments.The primary garment is a form-fitting, full-body bodysuit featuring long sleeves and an open, cut-out midriff construction. It consists of a structured, padded underwire bra top with molded cups, connected via intricate, cascading draped pearl body chains to a high-waisted, high-cut bottom piece. The fabric is a smooth, matte white synthetic material with strong shape retention, heavily overlaid with vertical and curved rows of multi-sized embroidered pearls. The long, skin-tight sleeves feature open-shoulder cutouts framed by rigid, structured epaulet-style shoulder caps, fully encrusted with pearls.Adorning the neckline is a prominent, wide choker collar composed of closely stacked horizontal rows of pearls that tapers upwards, finished with suspended teardrop pearl pendants. Additional accessories include a traditional tikka-style forehead ornament featuring a central filigree teardrop medallion with a hanging pearl, and matching teardrop pearl drop earrings.
