OUTFIT: outfit features a dark, alternative streetwear composition with a strong utilitarian and subcultural influence. The palette is almost entirely monochrome, dominated by deep black and charcoal tones, with tiny pops of red and white serving as sharp visual accents.The primary outerwear layer is a highly voluminous, cropped flight bomber jacket made of a slick, water-resistant black nylon. It is built with a low ribbed collar, a central gold-toned zipper, a rectangular black logo patch on the left chest, and a signature red utility tag hanging from a zippered sleeve pocket. For the lower body, the wearer sports a pair of loose, straight-leg jeans in a faded charcoal black denim. The pants feature an intentional hybrid design, accented by full-length, vertical white athletic triple-stripes stitched directly down the side seams from the waistband to the hem.Footwear consists of rugged, heavy-soled combat boots crafted from thick, matte black leather. They have a high-top shaft reaching above the ankle, a reinforced cap toe, prominent exposed white welt stitching, and long black laces. Visible accessories include a matte black digital watch with a matching segmented link bracelet.
