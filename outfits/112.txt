OUTFIT: outfit presents a sleek, monochromatic cyberpunk-inspired aesthetic entirely in black and dark grey tones, utilizing contrasting textures of sheer mesh, glossy leather, and metallic hardware. The visual composition centers on a tight, form-fitting upper body with high transparency, juxtaposed against a heavy, asymmetrical leather harness and structured, high-gloss lower garments.The base top is a sheer, ultra-thin dark grey mesh long-sleeve shirt with a skin-tight fit. It features a simple mock-neck collar and a raw, jaggedly torn asymmetric cropped hemline that leaves the midriff exposed. Layered directly over the left shoulder is a single-sleeved black leather shrug. This structured piece has a padded, angular shoulder and a slim sleeve ending mid-forearm, embellished with small silver metal grommets and lace-up details along its length.Securing this sleeve is a complex black leather body harness. It wraps over both shoulders with wide straps, featuring a prominent silver metal buckle closure across the upper chest and a fitted collar ring connected to a long, delicate silver chain that drapes down the front. The right arm features an independent, forearm-length black leather gauntlet with a fingerless glove design, detailed with parallel straps and silver studs. The left hand is covered by a standard fitted black leather glove.The lower half consists of slim-fit, high-waisted black trousers made from a glossy, coated material resembling vinyl or polished leather. The waistband is decorated with a series of silver metal safety pins arranged horizontally. These trousers tuck into knee-high black leather boots with a smooth finish, structured shafts, and subtle pointed toes.
