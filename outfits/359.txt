OUTFIT: ensemble is a highly ornate, shimmering performance outfit drawing heavily from traditional Middle Eastern belly dance attire, specifically a modern, fantasy-inspired Bedlah costume. The overall color palette is dominated by iridescent champagnes, metallic silvers, and warm golds that catch the light through a dense covering of sequins and metallic threads. Visual focus is shared equally between the structured, glittering bustier and the dramatic, voluminous cape-like veil flowing around the silhouette.The upper-body garment is a heavily structured, molded underwire bra top featuring a sweet-heart neckline and ultra-thin, barely-visible shoulder straps. It is thickly encrusted with silver sequins and metallic embroidery, with short, delicate strands of silver beads draped from the bottom edge of the cups. Separate, tight-fitting mesh sleeve bands wrap around the forearms, similarly covered in a dense pattern of gold and silver glitter, leaving the shoulders and upper arms bare. The lower-body garment is a floor-length mermaid-style skirt that sits low on the hips. It features a rigid, thickly embellished waistband or hip belt that mirrors the silver embroidery of the bra, decorated with dangling beaded swags. The skirt is crafted from a sheer, lightweight metallic fabric with a high side slit, offering low coverage while retaining a fluid drape that pools around the floor. Wrapping behind the figure is a massive, billowy veil made of a fluid, semi-translucent textile completely saturated with fine silver and iridescent glitter, creating immense volume and a dramatic, caped silhouette.Accessories include a silver filigree tikka resting on the forehead, matched with long, ornate crystal drop earrings and a chunky, layered V-shaped bib necklace encrusted with rhinestones.
