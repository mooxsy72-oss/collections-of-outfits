OUTFIT: bridal ensemble reinvents traditional wedding wear by contrasting a classic ballgown silhouette with an extreme, avant-garde high-low structure. The palette is stark, solid monochromatic ivory white, lacking any printed patterns.The gown features a heavily structured, form-fitting strapless corset bodice with a shallow sweetheart neckline, exposing the shoulders, collarbones, and arms. It is constructed from thick, high-shine satin fabric that provides rigid shape retention along vertical princess seams.The attached skirt creates dramatic, asymmetric visual volume. It is severely cropped in the front into a micro-mini hemline that exposes the upper thighs, while expanding into an oversized, voluminous floor-length train at the sides and back. The massive volume is created by thick, heavy fabric folds gathered at the dropped waist. A large, stylized fabric rosette is sculpted directly into the satin on the right hip, adding sculptural texture.Underneath, the legs are fully covered by sheer ivory-white thigh-high boots or stockings. They are crafted from a delicate, lightweight floral lace overlay featuring scalloped edges at the top band. A single-strand white pearl choker sits tightly around the neck.
