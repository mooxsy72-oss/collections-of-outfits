OUTFIT: outfit presents a dark-toned academic ensemble influenced by the modern "dark academia" and schoolcore aesthetics. The dominant color palette features deep charcoal grey and solid black, punctuated by a vibrant secondary pop of light sky blue. Visual focus is split between the oversized, layered proportions of the torso and the structured, geometric pleating of the lower half.The base layer is an oversized, long-sleeved dress shirt made from a smooth, lightweight woven cotton fabric in solid light sky blue. It features a stiff, sharp pointed collar buttoned to the throat and relaxed, dropped shoulders with voluminous, wide sleeves. The shirt remains untucked, with its curved, asymmetrical shirttail hem extending past the waist. Layered directly over it is a chunky, mid-weight cable-knit sweater vest in charcoal grey. The vest has a boxy, relaxed fit, a deep V-neckline, and thick ribbed trim along the neck, armholes, and straight waistband.The lower-body garment is a charcoal grey pleated mini skirt made from a structured, mid-weight twill fabric. It features sharp, uniform knife pleats that add structured volume, ending in a straight hemline mid-thigh. Mid-calf length, matte black cotton crew socks cover the lower legs.Footwear consists of chunky black leather loafers featuring a high-shine finish, a rounded toe box, a distinct penny-keeper strap across the vamp, and a thick, rugged rubber platform lug sole.Accessories include a matte black silk necktie knotted at the collar, featuring a small, metallic silver inverted triangle logo plaque near the knot. A black leather crescent-shaped shoulder bag sits tucked under the arm, detailed with heavy silver-toned studs, buckles, zippers, and a dangling rectangular pouch. Dark black rimless sunglasses are worn on the face, and small, chunky silver hoop earrings adorn the ears.
