OUTFIT: a winter-themed ensemble belongs to the Japanese Lolita fashion tradition, specifically Sweet Lolita, characterized by its heavily structured, doll-like silhouette, whimsical animal motif, and dense ornamental detailing. The color distribution is predominantly pastel pink, accented with stark white plush trims and secondary dusty-rose accents.The primary garment is an ultra-voluminous, mid-thigh length bell-shaped dress. It features a fitted, high-waisted corset-style bodice with a scalloped lace-trimmed upper edge and a central pink fabric bow. The short skirt achieves its extreme, cartoonish volume through dense fabric gathering at the waist, supported by internal petticoats. It is made of a heavy, smooth cotton or twill fabric with exceptional stiffness and shape retention. The skirt's hem is finished with a thin band of white lace, a wide band of dense, thick white faux fur, and two symmetrical dusty-rose fabric bows resting on round faux-fur patches.Layered over the dress is a short, structured capelet with an attached oversized hood featuring long, floppy rabbit ears with pink fabric linings. The capelet has a flared silhouette that covers the shoulders and chest completely, closing at the neck with two stacked pink bows. It is crafted from the same heavy pink fabric, heavily trimmed with thick white faux fur along the bottom edge, hood opening, and ear borders. Four small, round white faux-fur pom-poms dangle from pink cords across the front waistline.
