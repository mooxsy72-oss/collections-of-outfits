OUTFIT: a fantasy-coquette ensemble fuses historical Victorian corset structures with modern avant-garde bridal textures. The dominant color is ivory white, contrasted by a pale yellow bodice patterned with pink and green floral clusters.The silhouette centers on an extremely cinched, rigid front-lacing corset that creates an exaggerated hourglass shape. This sleeveless, strapless bodice features a sweetheart neckline, a pointed drop-waist hem, and crystal-beaded fringe dripping from the lower edge. Underneath, an asymmetric maxi skirt cascades to the floor, combining heavy cream lace with a sheer, lightweight tulle underlayer, absolutely translucent and weightless. The skirt tightly hugs the hips before flaring out, exposing portions of the lower leg through translucent fabric while trailing a delicate lace train behind.An intricate lace choker collar wraps tightly around the neck, while dainty pearl strands drape loosely across the upper arms. Pink pointed-toe satin pumps with silver embellished straps anchor the look. The construction relies entirely on structured, heavy fabrics for the bodice to retain its stiff shape, contrasted against the fluid drape and delicate transparency of the embroidered lace layers below.
