OUTFIT: athletic outfit aligns with the same post-Soviet subcultural streetwear tradition as the previous look, characterized by uniform sportswear styling. The color distribution is heavily dominated by deep black, with white serving as the secondary contrast accent color.The primary layer consists of a two-piece tracksuit. The top is a zip-up track jacket with a relaxed, standard fit, long raglan sleeves, and a built-in hood worn pulled up over the head. The matching track pants feature a straight-leg silhouette with a comfortable, relaxed drape through the thighs and calves, pooling slightly over the footwear. Both pieces are constructed from a smooth, mid-weight synthetic polyester knit fabric that maintains its shape while providing flexibility.The major visual decoration consists of three parallel, high-contrast white stripes running down the full outer length of each sleeve and trouser leg. This linear pattern breaks the solid black silhouette. The garments offer full body coverage, completely shielding the neck, torso, arms, and legs. On the feet, low-top, light gray retro-style athletic sneakers featuring a prominent white "N" logo on the side add a contrasting textural and color element to the dark, unified ensemble.
