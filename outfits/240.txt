OUTFIT: ensemble fuses traditional Chinese cheongsam elements with stylized, exaggerated anime fantasy design, highlighted by oversized sleeves and asymmetric structural cutouts. The dominant color is muted sage green, accented by stark secondary white straps, ribbons, and closures.The primary garment is a modified mini cheongsam dress tailored from structured, mid-weight green cotton or canvas with moderate stiffness. It features a high mandarin collar secured by three white frog closures over an open chest cutout. The dress is short, form-fitting, and highly geometric, leaving the midriff, hips, and entire leg area largely exposed via deep side slits and a diamond-shaped waist cutout held together by a white intersecting belt.The separate, detached sleeves dominate the silhouette with intentionally extreme, oversized volume. They attach below the shoulder with white straps, tapering into massive, bell-shaped forearms crisscrossed with thick white lacing through large metal grommets, terminating in gathered ruffle cuffs with long, trailing white ribbons.Accessories include asymmetric leg wear: one loose green calf-warmer with a frog closure, and one leg wrapped fully in crisscrossed white ribbons. Footwear consists of chunky white platform shoes with thick beige soles.
