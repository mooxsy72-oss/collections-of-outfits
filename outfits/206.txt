OUTFIT: outfit belongs to the Japanese Lolita fashion subculture, specifically Sweet Lolita, characterized by its heavily tiered, bell-shaped petticoat-supported skirt, extensive lace trims, and prominent satin bows. The color scheme is pastel, dominated by soft pink and pale cream/ivory, with no distinct graphic patterns.
The primary garment is a sleeveless, fitted JSK (jumper skirt) style dress featuring a corset-style laced bodice over the ribs, thick shoulder straps, and a square neckline. The mid-thigh length skirt achieves extreme volume through multiple layers of gathered, lightweight organza and satin tiers edged with delicate white lace.
Detached, dramatically oversized bell sleeves flare widely from the mid-forearm, matching the ivory shade of the dress's base layer, leaving the upper arms and shoulders fully exposed.
Accessories include a matching pale ivory lace choker necklace adorned with a small pink satin bow. On the lower body, the outfit leaves the thighs exposed, transitioning to white knee-high socks topped with dense, voluminous ruffles. The footwear consists of chunky, platform white Mary Jane style shoes wrapped in crisscrossing buckled straps that secure up the lower leg.
