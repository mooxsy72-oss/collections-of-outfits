OUTFIT: avant-garde outfit combines elements of haute couture and dark romanticism. Off-white is the dominant color, paired with a secondary deep black. A scattered polka-dot pattern features on the footwear.The core is an asymmetrical corseted dress. The bodice is a translucent, boned off-white corset. It has a strapless, sweetheart neckline obscured by voluminous ruffles. The midriff is covered, while shoulders, arms, and collarbones are exposed.The skirt is an ultra-voluminous bubble silhouette. It is crafted from heavily gathered off-white tulle. This gives it extreme shape retention and structural rigidity. The left side cascades down past the knee in an elongated drape. Large, realistic fabric rosettes are nestled into the tulle layers. This exposes the right thigh and leg completely.Accessories include an oversized, high-set collar. It is made of reflective, faceted silver material. The footwear consists of knee-high black velvet boots. They have a structured, wide-shaft silhouette. The fabric is adorned with an all-over pattern of tiny, glittering silver rhinestones.
