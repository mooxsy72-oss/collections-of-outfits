OUTFIT: Outfit draws inspiration from traditional Slavic, Baltic, and Central Asian folk clothing traditions, supported by the high-collared kaftan-style bodice, dense geometric embroidery, heavily patterned textiles, and a woven sash with dangling elements.The visual composition relies on earthy, muted tones dominated by deep brick red, maroon, and ochre, balanced by detailed accents of cream and faded blue. Visual focus is drawn to the highly detailed, densely embroidered central torso panel and vertical zipper trim.The upper garment is a structured, heavy cotton or wool-blend bodice with a straight, full-coverage mandarin collar and straight-cut sleeves. The collar, shoulders, and chest panel are covered in dense, small-scale geometric and floral folk embroidery using red, white, and gold threads, framed by vertical patterned woven trim. The sleeves are a muted maroon, covered in a subtle, faded paisley and floral print, ending in wide, stiff cuffs that match the dense chest embroidery.The lower garment is a full-length, fluidly draping red kirtle or skirt that gathers heavily at the natural waist. It features a medium-scale, faded cream and gold botanical pattern distributed uniformly down to the straight, unadorned hemline.The silhouette is defined by a structured, close-fitting upper torso contrasting with the loose, soft drape of the lower skirt. A narrow, woven textile sash in cream and brown wraps tightly around the waist, knotted with long, thin, fringed tassels and dark brown bird feathers that dangle down the front center of the skirt.
