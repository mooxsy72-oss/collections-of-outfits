OUTFIT: an iridescent mermaid costume features a lavender, lilac, and pale blue color palette with extensive metallic chain draping and crystal embellishments that command the most visual attention. The outfit consists of a highly structured shell-like bralette, an asymmetric draped hip wrap, and an intricate, integrated body chain harness.The bralette features two molded, rigid cups shaped like scalloped sea shells with a glossy, pearlescent finish. It fits closely as a strapless demi-bra with a plunging center gore adorned by a square blue gemstone. The sweetheart neckline and lower rim are trimmed with delicate gold filigree, while draping gold chains loop beneath the cups.The asymmetric hip wrap is constructed from lightweight, sheer, and airy tulle and organza fabrics in soft lavender and silver tones. It features a low-rise waistline that sits diagonally across the hips, leaving the midriff entirely open. The fabric is heavily gathered on the left hip, cascading down into a tattered, multi-layered high-low hemline with a fluid drape. It is heavily decorated with 3D fabric flowers, sequined starfish motifs, metallic embroidery, and dangling crystal teardrops.An intricate white-gold or silver body chain harness serves as the primary accessory. It begins as a multi-layered choker necklace studded with oval blue and clear rhinestones, transitions into a delicate web of chest chains, and extends down the torso into long, sweeping drapes that frame the waist and connect back to the skirt's waistband.
