OUTFIT: ensemble blends a high-fantasy aesthetic with delicate romanticism, contrasting rigid late-medieval plate armor against a semi-sheer dress. The dominant color is translucent cream-white, contrasted by the secondary metallic silver-gray of polished steel armor.The primary garment is a sleeveless, form-fitting lace slip dress. It features a scoop neckline, thin shoulder straps, and a short, upper-thigh length hemline that cascades into a sheer, asymmetrical ruffled train pooling behind. The fabric is a lightweight, delicate floral lace with a high-density pattern throughout, displaying low shape retention and a soft, fluid drape. It provides minimal body coverage, leaving the arms, left shoulder, collarbones, and thighs fully exposed.The silhouette's volume is heavily distorted by asymmetric, oversized steel plate armor components acting as the primary focal points. A rigid, polished metal gorget encircles the neck. Affixed to the right side is an articulated, bulky pauldron (shoulder guard) featuring detailed floral relief engravings, which connects via a couter (elbow guard) to a tubular vambrace on the forearm. The left arm is mostly bare, clad only in an isolated, engraved steel vambrace on the forearm.
