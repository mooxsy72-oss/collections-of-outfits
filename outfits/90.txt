OUTFIT: fantasy costume is based on a heavily modified, stylized Korean hanbok, identified by the distinctive wrap-front jeogori (jacket) structure and contrasting neckband.The dominant color is monochromatic black, with secondary accents of crisp white and metallic silver. The primary garment is an oversized, floor-length black robe or long jacket crafted from a lightweight, subtly textured woven fabric with a slight satin sheen. It features crisp, structured drapes and wide, voluminous sleeves. The neckline is constructed with a contrasting white collar band that crosses over the chest. The robe is styled completely open on the right side, deliberately exposing the wearer's shoulder, chest, torso, and thigh, while the left arm and side remain fully covered by the voluminous fabric.Securing the waist is a thick, structural fabric sash in matching black, overlaid with a prominent, wide woven white belt. The central focal point is a large, highly decorative circular metallic silver medallion with intricate relief carvings, positioned directly at the center of the waist. Hanging from this belt are long, decorative white cords and dense tassels that extend down the front of the skirt. A solid black fabric blindfold wraps horizontally around the upper head, serving as a secondary accessory.
