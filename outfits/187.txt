OUTFIT: avant-garde ensemble belongs to a high-fashion fantasy tradition, combining ecclesiastical angelic motifs with futuristic, structured tailoring. The color palette is strictly monochromatic, dominated by a stark, crisp white, with secondary metallic gold tones defining the rigid, architectural accessories.The inner layer features a heavily cropped, long-sleeved top with a fitted turtleneck collar. Constructed from a thick, opaque, structured white textile, it retains a smooth, rigid shape that cuts off sharply below the chest, exposing the entire midriff and navel. The matching white trousers are tailored with a mid-rise waist and a relaxed, straight-leg silhouette that tapers slightly toward the ankles, maintaining a clean line with minimal fabric drape.Providing dramatic visual volume, an oversized, floor-length cape-like duster coat is layered over the top. This outer piece is made of a sheer, featherweight white organza fabric adorned with a subtle, scattered white botanical embroidery pattern. The coat features intentionally exaggerated, wide bell sleeves that drape heavily past the hands.Footwear consists of bulky white leather lace-up boots with thick, deeply lugged platform soles. The silhouette is anchored by a massive gold metal halo headpiece featuring long, radiating metallic spikes, paired with a matching gold chain mail face veil that completely covers the lower face. A delicate body chain wraps the torso over the top.
