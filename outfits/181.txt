OUTFIT: post-apocalyptic, winter-ready survival outfit features structured layering and heavily distressed elements designed for cold utility. The dominant colors are muted olive-green and faded denim-blue, supported by secondary tones of charcoal-gray and muddy brown, with a prominent spattering of dried mud serving as the primary visual texture.An olive-green canvas utility jacket dominates the upper body silhouette. It features a relaxed fit, a pointed collar, a hidden button placket, and two large buttoned chest pockets. The heavy-weight, stiff cotton canvas holds its shape rigidly, resisting soft drapes. Layered immediately beneath is a charcoal-gray fleece hoodie; its fabric hood extends outward over the jacket collar. The sleeves cover the arms fully down to the wrists, leaving no skin exposed.The lower body is defined by slim-fit, mid-rise jeans in a faded medium-blue denim. The mid-weight fabric conforms tightly to the legs and bunches at the ankles, heavily splattered with white and beige dried mud up to the knees. On the feet are rugged, worn brown leather combat boots with thick black rubber soles and matching brown laces.Accessories include dark olive-green work gloves, a structured charcoal tactical backpack compressing the shoulders, and a brown leather holster strapped to the right hip.
