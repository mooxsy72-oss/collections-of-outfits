OUTFIT: ensemble draws from fantasy stage costume design, blending elements of a showgirl aesthetic with dark cupid or fallen angel imagery, supported by the prominent matching wings and theatrical textures. The monochromatic color palette consists entirely of a deep, uniform burgundy red, relying heavily on contrasting textures rather than varied hues.Dominating the look is a highly structured, strapless mini-dress featuring a tight corsetry fit. The bodice is constructed from stiff, form-retaining panels covered in dense, shimmering metallic sequins that capture light sharply. It features a sweetheart neckline, a pointed basque waist, and exposes the collarbones, shoulders, and arms. The lower skirt portion drops from the hips into an extremely high-volume, spherical tier of dense, fluffy marabou feathers. This layered feather structure flairs out horizontally, significantly exaggerating the hip silhouette before ending at the upper thigh.Spreading from the shoulder blades are two large, symmetrical prop angel wings made of stiff, layered plumage that curve upward. The legs are covered from the lower thigh down by matching tall, over-the-knee boots crafted from matte synthetic leather. These boots feature a skin-tight fit, structured high heels, and a front lace-up closure with metallic eyelets running the entire length. A thin, matching fabric band functions as a simple choker necklace.
