OUTFIT: ensemble combines elements of historical Victorian corsetry with fantasy mermaid aesthetics, defined by a rigid, structured torso and a fluid, flared lower leg. The dominant color is ivory white, contrasted by a secondary pastel blue core.The primary upper garment is an overbust corset constructed from structured, mid-weight pastel blue satin or velvet. It features a pointed basque waistline, a deep-V neckline framed by voluminous, gathered white lace ruffles, and draped pearl strands along the lower ribs. It heavily shapes the torso, leaving the shoulders, collarbones, and upper chest entirely exposed.The lower garment is a high-waisted, floor-length mermaid skirt made of smooth, heavy ivory satin. It fits tightly across the hips and thighs, then dramatically flares at the mid-calf into a sheer, lightweight white lace flounce with an asymmetrical hemline.Accessories include matching sheer white lace opera gloves that cover the forearms, leaving the upper arms bare, and a multi-stranded pearl choker that tightly wraps the neck. The visual weight is heavily concentrated in the structured blue waist and the contrasting flared lace hemline, creating a dramatic hourglass silhouette.
