OUTFIT: ensemble evokes a high-fantasy, princely or elven-inspired aristocratic aesthetic, blending historical Renaissance doublet elements with fantasy court styles. The color composition is anchored by a deep forest green and an off-white or cream base, accented with extensive gold ornamentation. The vibrant green vest and its contrasting metallic gold embroidery draw the immediate focus, while the flowing, draped waist sash adds structural volume and motion.The foundation layer is a long-sleeved cream dress shirt crafted from lightweight, fluid linen or cotton fabric. It features a tailored, small standing band collar and a partial front placket adorned with subtle gold thread details. The long sleeves fit comfortably along the arms and terminate in fitted cuffs featuring matching gold scrollwork embroidery at the wrists. Over the shirt sits a highly structured, sleeveless vest or jerkin made from thick forest-green wool or velvet. The vest features a deeply plunging, sharp V-neckline outlined with a thick border of intricate gold floral filigree embroidery, which continues densely across the entire front panels. The lower hemline of the vest terminates in a sharp point.The lower body is dressed in slim, tapered cream trousers in the same coloration as the shirt, tailored from a crisp, mid-weight woven material that gathers slightly at the knees and thighs. Wrapped tightly around the natural waist is a wide, dark green silk or satin cummerbund-style sash that layers beneath the pointed vest. Tied off to the left side, a secondary, longer panel of the green sash draping down past the knee, finished with a decorative gold fringe trim at its rectangular edge.Footwear comprises tall, structured riding boots crafted from matte dark brown leather. The boots feature a rounded toe, a stiff shaft that extends just below the knee, and a decorative gold embroidered scrolling pattern along the scalloped top rim. A multi-layered gold chain necklace serves as the singular visible accessory, draping across the upper chest within the open V-neckline of the vest.
