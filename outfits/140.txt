OUTFIT: avant-garde ensemble references European Baroque court dress through an extremely exaggerated, high-low silhouette where the lower half is defined by a massive, cloud-like explosion of fabric. The dominant color is stark white, with secondary sky blue, metallic gold, and solid black accents.The upper body features a structured white poplin shirt with a classic collar and caricature-like leg-of-mutton sleeves that burst into voluminous puffs. Layered over it is a rigid, tight-fitting corset featuring a blue floral print and thick, sculpted metallic gold scrollwork borders.The absolute focal point of the outfit is the colossal, hyper-voluminous high-low skirt. Constructed from countless tiers of stiff, sheer white tulle netting, the fabric is gathered with extreme density to create immense, structural visual volume that stands out aggressively from the body. It forms an ultra-short, highly puffed horizontal bounce at the front mid-thigh—completely exposing the legs—before cascading into a sweeping, multi-layered train that dramatically pooling on the floor behind.Accessories include chunky, solid black mid-calf combat boots that ground the massive airy volume above, a short dark chain necklace, and long silver dangle earrings.
