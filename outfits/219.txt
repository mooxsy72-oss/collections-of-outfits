OUTFIT: modern streetwear outfit relies on a solid, muted color palette of dark brown, black, and light beige, completely lacking patterns. The silhouette is defined by relaxed, intentionally oversized, and voluminous proportions.The outer layer is a heavy, structured bomber-style jacket in dark brown, featuring a wide, flat pointed collar, dropped shoulders, and relaxed, roomy sleeves. Made from a thick, stiff woven fabric like brushed cotton or canvas, it retains an angular shape with minimal drape. It features a silver metal zipper closure at the front and a cropped waist length. Beneath the jacket is a light beige henley shirt made from a soft, lightweight knit jersey fabric that drapes loosely over the torso. It features a round neckline with a partial button placket, exposing a small portion of the lower neck.The bottom half is dominated by high-waisted, loose-fitting wide-leg trousers in solid black. They feature deep front pleats that create extra fabric volume, cascading straight down into puddling folds over the footwear. The legs are completely covered. On the feet are chunky black leather loafers with a thick, robust sole and rounded toe. A black leather shoulder bag with a wide fabric strap hangs over the left shoulder.
