OUTFIT: a highly distressed, layered composition dominated by earthy tones of olive drab, muddy brown, and charcoal gray. Visual attention centers on the heavy textures of filth, tears, and rugged utility layers distributed across a form-fitting silhouette.The base layer is an olive-drab, lightweight cotton tank top with a deep scoop neckline and a body-hugging fit. It is heavily soiled with dark stains and mud. Over this sits an asymmetric, sleeveless utility vest made of heavy, structured canvas in a faded olive-brown shade. The vest features tattered, raw-edged shoulder openings, a high standing collar, and an open front that exposes the tank top beneath.On the lower body, slim-fit combat trousers in dark, muddy brown canvas are heavily stained and torn. They feature side cargo pockets, tactical knee reinforcements, and a thick, utilitarian brown waist belt. The trouser cuffs are tightly tucked into rugged, high-top tactical combat boots wrapped in matching tattered fabric bindings at the ankles.Accessories include fingerless dark gray tactical gloves and a narrow black leather strap worn diagonally across the torso from the left shoulder to the right hip. The entire ensemble relies on tight layering, frayed fabric edges, and dense layers of simulated dirt to create a worn, high-texture survivalist aesthetic.
