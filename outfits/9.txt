OUTFIT:  a highly structured, historical-inspired ensemble featuring an ornate jacket and a voluminous, tiered maxi skirt. The form-fitting jacket has long, slim sleeves ending in dramatic, flared ruffles, a high standing collar, and a cinched corset-like waist construction with a pointed hem. A heavy, densely beaded dark red scarf or trim wraps around the high neckline and drapes down the front bodice. The fabric throughout is a structured textile featuring a repeating pattern of golden-yellow floral bouquets on vertical stripes of cream and deep red, interspersed with solid dark red paneling. The full-length skirt is heavily gathered at the waist, creating a wide, dramatic silhouette with a bustled layer at the back. All fabrics display a high-saturation, deep red hue contrasted against muted cream and bright ochre yellow patterns. Accessories include matching dark red beaded drop earrings.
