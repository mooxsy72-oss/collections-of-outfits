OUTFIT: outfit blends elements of Rococo-inspired historical costuming with Japanese Classic and Gothic Lolita fashion traditions, supported by the prominent use of a structured corset bodice, decorative garter straps, pannier-like hip volume, and a dramatic high-low hemline. The visual composition is anchored by a monochromatic palette of cream and ivory, accented with muted dusty-pink ribbons. Visual attention is primarily drawn to the highly structured, hourglass waistline and the contrast between the short front underskirt and the long, scalloped lace train.The primary full-body garment consists of a tightly fitted, structured corset-style bodice seamlessly integrated with a voluminous, layered high-low skirt. The bodice features a square neckline trimmed with fine ivory lace, pintuck pleating across the chest, and a wide panel of beige floral lace. It is heavily boned to create an exaggerated hourglass silhouette, extending into a curved, pointed hem over the hips. Dusty-pink satin ribbons decorate the shoulder straps and anchor the functional front criss-cross lacing at the lower hips, which terminates in two long, adjustable ruched garter straps with metallic clips hanging down the front. The attached skirt features an open-front overskirt that pulls back to reveal dense, multi-layered ivory tulle ruffles on the short front underskirt. The overskirt cascades down the sides and back into a floor-length train made of semi-sheer, lightweight cream mesh, decorated with rows of inset lace trim and finished with a wide, scalloped floral lace edge.
