OUTFIT: fantasy outfit draws from Rococo court dress and modern Japanese Lolita fashion, utilizing a highly structured, hyper-feminine silhouette with extreme ruffling and layering. The color scheme is dominated by an ornate blue-and-white damask-patterned fabric and crisp white accents, creating a high-contrast palette.The core garment is a tight-fitting, rigid corset-bodice featuring a deeply scooped sweetheart neckline and a center-front lace-up placket. The shoulders and collarbones are completely exposed. Attached to the bodice are voluminous, multi-tiered puff sleeves that flare into wide lace ruffles at the elbow. The lower half features a dramatic, hyper-voluminous high-low skirt assembly. A short, highly gathered white ruffled underskirt sits at the upper thigh, heavily accented by large fabric flower rosettes. Cascading from the back waist are long, structural side-panniers made of the heavy blue damask fabric, lined with thick white lace ruffles that pool dramatically onto the floor.Beneath the skirt, sheer white mesh tights with dimensional floral embroidery cover the legs. Accessories include matching blue fabric flower corsages at the waist and ankles, sheer white floor-length fabric streamers from the back shoulders, and black lace-up stiletto heels.
