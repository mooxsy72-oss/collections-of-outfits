OUTFIT: This ensemble blends Victorian-era aesthetics with avant-garde fantasy haute couture, defined by a hyper-exaggerated hourglass silhouette and a dramatic play of structured volume against fluid drape. The outfit covers the entire body except the hands and head.The visual focal point is a highly structured, rigid corset-style bodice in metallic silver and cream. It features a extreme cinched waist, a pointed basque waistline, a row of front functional button-loop closures, and integrated molded cups with delicate silver filigree embroidery. This rigid structure layer over a sheer, lightweight cream mesh chemise that creates a high ruffled mock-neck collar and a translucent yoke covering the collarbones. The long sleeves feature voluminous, structured puff shoulders that taper down into fitted forearms, finishing with oversized, flared lace-trimmed cuffs.The lower half is dominated by an expansive, floor-length A-line overskirt made of heavy, high-shine satin in champagne-gold. The fabric exhibits dense, liquid-like draping and high shape retention, pooling heavily at the hem. Sweeping open panels reveal a scalloped, wide-set floral lace trim along the edges, layering over a structured under-petticoat that maximizes lower-body volume.
