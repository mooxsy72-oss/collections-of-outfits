A vertical, triangular panel of off-white linen—matching the veil—hangs straight down from the browline, completely concealing the upper face, eyes, and nose. It features a border of rust-red geometric embroidery along its pointed lower edge. Made from a matching stiff off-white linen, it covers the head and falls straight down past the elbows. It features thick bands of rust-red geometric cross-stitch embroidery along the front vertical edges and horizontal hems, with a small matching triangular panel masking the central face area.The visual volume is dominated by complex, tiered neck and head accessories. Cascading down the front is a elaborate multi-strand necklace ensemble made of chunky wooden beads, small animal skulls, and skeletal bones.
