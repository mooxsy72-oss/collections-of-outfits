OUTFIT: coquette-aesthetic ensemble features a monochrome off-white and cream color palette, relying on heavily layered lace, sheer textiles, and delicate satin ribbon accents to build romantic, soft volume.The uppermost layer is a cropped, sheer mesh robe or capelet with an open front and no closure. It dominates the upper silhouette with dramatic, voluminous bell sleeves that flare wide below the elbow, ending in tiered ruffles adorned with floral embroidery trims. Underneath, a structured lace-overlay corset bustier acts as the focal piece, featuring a straight strapless neckline, molded underwire cups, vertical boning channels, and a tight, body-conforming fit that exposes the collarbones and upper chest. At the waist, a thin cream satin ribbon belt ties into a central bow.The lower half consists of high-waisted shorts featuring a fitted upper section and a highly voluminous, tiered lower hem. Three horizontal layers of gathered lace ruffles flare outward over the upper thighs, mirroring the sleeve ruffles. A thin satin ribbon choker with a front bow completes the accessories. All edges—including the sleeves, shorts hem, and corset top—are finished with intricate eyelash or scalloped lace embroidery.
