OUTFIT: fantasy-infused avant-garde gown features an organic, asymmetrical silhouette dominated by an intricately structured, fibrous material resembling dense, tangled roots or cobwebs. The primary color palette consists of off-white, translucent ivory, and stark charcoal black.The main garment is a strapless, floor-length, corseted gown with a dramatic high-thigh split on the wearer's left side. The bodice is structured, rigid, and tightly fitted, presenting an extreme, elongated hourglass proportion. It features a plunging, irregular sweetheart neckline and a dropped waist, entirely exposing the shoulders, collarbones, and one full leg.The fabric on the bodice and left leg consists of an ultra-textured, open-work mesh of thick, intertwined white threads that form a hollow, sculptural cage. Layered over the right hip and trailing into a massive, voluminous train is a lightweight, flowing ivory organza skirt. This secondary fabric drapes in fluid, voluminous folds, heavily overlaid with a contrasting, dark charcoal pattern of branch-like veins that cascade downward. The visual volume is created by the stark contrast between the rigid, webbed bodice cage and the expansive, gathered fabric of the trailing skirt layer.
