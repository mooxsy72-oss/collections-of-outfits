OUTFIT: outfit belongs to a fantasy witch tradition, heavily borrowing elements from the traditional Chinese cheongsam or qipao, indicated by its high mandarian-style collar, asymmetrical front closure, and high side slits. It blends deep violet-purple, crisp white, and pale lavender with ornate gold filigree and dark lace accents. Visual attention is drawn primarily to the contrasted white bustier panel, the sculptural fabric flowers, and the high-slit skirt.The main garment is a form-fitting full-body dress featuring a white, gathered sweetheart bustier paired with a structured, deep purple midsection and skirt. It has a high purple mandarin collar that fastens at the neck with a gold oval keyhole brooch, transitioning into short, translucent lavender bell sleeves with jagged, gold-trimmed hems. The fitted skirt splits high at both hips into narrow, pointed front and back panels that drape downward, finished with gold embroidered filigree along the front panel's V-shaped edge. Beneath the dress, sheer black thigh-high stockings with wide, intricate dark grey lace bands cover the legs, secured to the dress by black criss-cross straps at the upper thighs. A flowing, lightweight white capelet with sharp, pointed tails splits down the back, draping loosely to knee length.Footwear consists of deep purple pointed-toe pumps accented with cross-crossed gold straps over the instep, paired with a wide, jeweled anklet hosting a dangling purple teardrop gem. Accessories feature dark purple elbow-length gloves with pointed cuffs and a large, structured purple witch hat. The hat has a wide, wavy brim lined with pale purple fabric displaying faint circular sigils, a leather belt band with a gold buckle, and a prominent sculpted purple rose tucked into the crown, complemented by a larger cream-colored rose nestled at the right hip.
