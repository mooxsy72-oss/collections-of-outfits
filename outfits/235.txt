OUTFIT: avant-garde, three-piece tailoring ensemble subverts classic tailoring with an theatrical, dark-fantasy aesthetic, utilizing a monochromatic gray, black, and white color palette.The primary layer features a single-breasted blazer in a thick, highly structured gray and white glen plaid tweed fabric. It has classic notch lapels, straight sleeves, a relaxed fit, raw distressed hems, and prominent patch pockets. Beneath sits a matching plaid tweed vest layered over a crisp, white dress shirt and a coordinating slim plaid necktie with a metallic tie clip. The lower half features asymmetrical layering, combining straight-leg cropped plaid tweed trousers with a dramatic, flowing, ankle-length pleated black kilt skirt that introduces contrasting movement and volume.The silhouette is defined by the rigid, structured upper tailoring juxtaposed against the fluid, asymmetrical lower skirt layer, providing full body coverage.The major visual focal point is a theatrical, oversized rabbit mask crafted from woven gray fabric. It features towering, erect ears with deep central creases and extended wire whiskers. Matching gray plaid tweed gloves and dark gray textured oxford shoes with patterned mid-calf socks complete the look.
