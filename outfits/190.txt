OUTFIT: ensemble belongs to a dark fantasy clothing tradition, combining haute couture mermaid gown architecture with demonic, bat-like creature anatomy. The monochromatic color palette is entirely pitch black, relying on the contrast between sheer, patterned textures and heavy fluid fabrics.The focal garment is a floor-length mermaid-silhouette gown with a dramatic floor-sweeping train. It features a plunging V-neckline that extends down to the waist, skin-tight long sleeves ending in clawed hand extensions, and a high thigh slit on the left side, exposing the leg. The upper bodice, sleeves, and hips are constructed from semi-sheer, lightweight lace featuring an intricate, organic web-like pattern. Sprouting from the shoulders are jagged, branch-like skeletal prosthetics. The lower skirt transitions at the mid-thigh into a dense, heavyweight matte jersey or crepe fabric that pools heavily on the floor, creating fluid drapes and a wide puddle train that balances the top-heavy silhouette.Dominating the upper silhouette are two massive, symmetrical, webbed bat-style wings attached at the back. These structures are built from a rigid, skeletal armature with curved, pointed talons at the tips, inset with the same sheer webbed lace fabric, creating immense visual volume.
